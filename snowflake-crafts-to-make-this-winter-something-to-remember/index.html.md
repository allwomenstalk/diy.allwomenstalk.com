# 17 Snowflake Crafts to Make This Winter Something to Remember ...

In many parts of the world, the first winter snow has already fallen, which makes it a great time to indulge in some snowflake crafts. Whether you do them with your kids, your friends or on your own, you'll end up with something beautiful to decorate with. Snowflake crafts are easy to execute and lovely to look at, so go ahead and use the ones on this list to inspire an entire winter of crafting. Don't forget to let me know how things turn out.

## 1. Popsicle Stick Snowflakes

![Popsicle Stick Snowflakes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ki/ay/zd8n9g2x_386x576.jpg)

Via [Our Winter Window Display: Popsicle ...](http://redirect.viglink.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 14. Crocheted Snowflake

## 15. String Them up

![String Them up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fm/zw/w8y0s6se_550x1468.jpg)

## 16. Pipe Cleaners with Beads

## 17. Noodle Snowflake Ornament

![Noodle Snowflake Ornament](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dh/il/kvrwfgtc_450x350.jpg)
