# The 17 Best Instagram Home Decor Accounts to Follow for Instant Inspiration

Are you looking to refresh your living space but don't know where to start? Finding the right inspiration is the first step toward creating a home you love. From minimalist chic to vibrant maximalism, the world of interior design is constantly evolving, and social media has become the ultimate gallery for fresh ideas. Getting bored of your home? Let's re-decorate with this gorgeous **Instagram home decor inspiration**!

| Style | Best For | Vibe |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Minimalist | Small Spaces | Clean & Airy |
| Bohemian | Cozy Living | Warm & Textural |
| Modern | Professional | Sleek & Polished |

## 1. @caitlinwilsondesign

Check out the official [Caitlin Wilson website](https://caitlinwilson.com/) for more elegant patterns. This account is a masterclass in sophisticated, feminine design that brings a touch of luxury to any room.

## 2. @jennykomenda

Jenny's approach to [home renovation](https://jennykomenda.com/) is both practical and stunning. She often shares DIY tips that make high-end looks accessible for everyone.

## 3. @em_henderson

Emily Henderson is a legend in the design world. Her [blog](https://stylebyemilyhenderson.com/) is a treasure trove of styling secrets and approachable design advice.

## 4. @brookewagnerdesign

For those who love a clean, coastal aesthetic, Brooke Wagner's portfolio is a must-see. Her designs often feature bright, open spaces and natural textures.

## 5. @ladolcevitablog

Paloma Contreras brings a modern take on traditional style. Her eye for detail and classic silhouettes makes her a favorite for timeless interiors.

## 6. @pencilandpaperco

This Nashville-based duo creates spaces that are full of personality and color. Their work is a great reminder that design should be fun and reflective of your own story.

## 7. @elledecor

As a leading authority in the industry, [Elle Decor](https://www.elledecor.com/) showcases the best in global design, from celebrity homes to cutting-edge trends.

## 8. @decoristofficial

Decorist makes interior design accessible through their online services. Their Instagram is packed with real-life room transformations that prove anyone can have a beautiful home.

## 9. @scoutandnimble

Scout & Nimble is a fantastic resource for both furniture and inspiration. They curate a beautiful feed that highlights various design styles and high-quality pieces.

## 10. @homedeconl

This account offers a peek into European design trends, focusing on cozy, livable spaces that don't sacrifice style for comfort.

- Focus on natural lighting to brighten up rooms.

- Incorporate indoor plants for a touch of greenery.

- Mix different textures like wood, metal, and fabric.

## 11. @homepolish

Homepolish was known for connecting clients with designers. Their feed remains a great source for seeing a wide variety of professionally designed homes.

## 12. @apartmenttherapy

[Apartment Therapy](https://www.apartmenttherapy.com/) is the go-to for small space living. They provide endless tips on how to make the most of every square inch.

## 13. @onekingslane

One Kings Lane offers a mix of vintage and modern pieces. Their Instagram is a great place to learn how to layer different eras for a curated look.

## 14. @ashleytstark

Ashley Stark Kenner, the Creative Director of STARK, shares incredible rug and carpet inspiration, showing how the right foundation can transform a room.

## 15. @luluandgeorgia

[Lulu and Georgia](https://www.luluandgeorgia.com/) is a favorite for trendy, high-quality home decor. Their feed is always up-to-date with the latest must-have items.

## 16. @1stdibs

For the truly unique and high-end, 1stdibs is the ultimate destination. Their Instagram features museum-quality furniture and art from around the world.

## 17. @mydomaine

MyDomaine provides a mix of home tours, lifestyle tips, and design trends, making it a well-rounded source for all things home.

Suggest your favorite home decor accounts! What accounts do you follow for inspiration?
