# 9 Incredibly Gorgeous DIY Earrings to Amp Up Your Spring Style

If you're looking for ways to update your look for the season, these fun DIY spring earrings are a perfect project. These designs showcase the season's hottest colors: shades of blue, orange, plum, and hints of marsala. Many of these projects also feature botanical elements that are sure to give your OOTD a dash of spring freshness.

## Stone and Brass Dreamcatchers

![Stone and Brass Dreamcatchers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qe/zr/gy98ppt15542ce6d43249624334827_600x400.jpg)

This pair of DIY spring earrings offers a fresh take on the classic dreamcatcher. Using only round brass elements and beads in oval and cylindrical shapes, you can create a cute piece of spring jewelry. For more inspiration, check out our guide on [how to make boho jewelry](https://diy.allwomenstalk.com/how-to-make-boho-jewelry/) to complement your new accessories.

## Cascading Jade Pair

![Cascading Jade Pair](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d7/fr/ryhj74zc5542ce6e8ba84988384779_600x291.jpg)

These pretty earrings showcase jade or marble beads in marsala. You can craft many fun accessories throughout the year featuring this versatile color. While it is a shade typically seen in colder months, these beads make it perfect for DIY spring jewelry.

## Boho Hoops

![Boho Hoops](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/an/p9/yjusrqah5542ce6f37ee9641417464_600x481.jpg)

How gorgeous are these DIY earrings? This lovely pair showcases beads in mint green, accented with fun metallic elements. To make this even more spring-appropriate, consider swapping the center teardrop bead for something in soft blue or marsala.
