# How to Give Your Closet a Major Makeover ...

If you're anything like me, your closets are a nightmare. I organize them, but it never lasts long. I think the trick is to make the closet a little bit pretty and a whole lot streamlined so that you're motivated to keep it neat and tidy. Of course, you'll have to teach everyone else in your house how to do the same. Here's some inspiration that I think will help you out.

## 1. Nursery Closet Makeover

![Nursery Closet Makeover](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dh/am/fgrob6rv5640ca9602ed0797551942_550x734.jpg)

Source: [Nursery Closet Update {Organization Ideas}](https://realitydaydream.com/nursery-closet-update-organization-ideas/)
