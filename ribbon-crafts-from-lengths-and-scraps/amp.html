Frequently Asked Questions

What are ribbon crafts?
Ribbon crafts are creative projects where you use ribbons to make decorations, accessories, or artwork. You can use new ribbons or scraps from other projects.
Do I need special tools to work with ribbons?
Most ribbon crafts require only simple tools like scissors, glue, and sometimes a needle and thread. Some might need more, like wire for shaping or a sewing machine, but it depends on the project.
Can I use any type of ribbon for my crafts?
Yes, you can use different types of ribbon depending on what you are making. Satin, grosgrain, and lace are some common choices, but feel free to experiment with what you have.
What can I make with ribbon scraps?
Ribbon scraps are great for small projects like hair accessories, embellishing cards, making jewelry, or decorating gift packages. Be creative and think of ways to use small pieces!
How do I prevent ribbon edges from fraying?
You can prevent fraying by using a lighter or a fray check liquid to seal the edges. Just be careful and use these methods in a well-ventilated area.
