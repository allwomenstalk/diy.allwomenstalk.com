# The Coolest Guide on How to Make a Wreath Using Flip Flops ...

Want to learn **how to make a wreath using flip flops**?

Not quite the creative type? Have an obsession with different flip flops for different outfits? Like Dollar Tree and/or WalMart? I have the craft for you...the ultimate summer wreath...a flip flop wreath!

You do not have to follow the below YouTube tutorial exactly. Make them bigger or smaller. Use what you want in your favorite colors or your favorite patterns, if you want to use flowers or not, etc.

I think you are going to like this video that teaches you **how to make a wreath using flip flops**.

TnT Kids & Home
Published on Jun 10, 2016

Out of all of the YouTube videos on this craft, I found this craft video to be the best. No complicated steps, no complicated supplies. It features easy to find supplies and easy to buy supplies. Cheap, attractive and perfect for the home, for this upcoming summer season.

Need some spare cash? Why not make a few of these wreaths and sell them? If you really do not have a person that you know of that would want to buy such wreaths, why not check out Etsy.com, Storenvy.com or Fiverr.com and sell your wreathes online? Hold a yard sale and sell them right in your own yard! You could have yourself a nice little side hustle.

A nice way to spend some quality "me" time. Turn on some music and get lost in the craft.

Have fun!
