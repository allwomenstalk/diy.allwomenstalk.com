# 10 Awesome NYC\-Inspired DIY Projects ...

Our list of [Paris-themed crafts](https://lifestyle.allwomenstalk.com/delightful-paris-inspired-diy-projects/) was a big hit so we're back with these fun NYC-inspired DIYs. The following **NYC-themed crafts** will give you great ways to upgrade your home decor. And yes, they are fantastic projects for those who are utterly and hopelessly in love with this vibrant city. Enjoy!

## 1. Skyline Shelving

_ How insanely cool is this? According to the designer, this NYC skyline shelving unit is for a kid's room but any adult who loves the city will find it hard to say no to this home improvement project. Check out the link to download the plan (in French).

## 2. Railing Pillow

![Railing Pillow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/09/23-560x371_560x371.jpg)

The lovely wrought iron railings of the Village, Chelsea, and Soho are very New York. Inspired by their form, you can make a beautiful pillow for your home. This is an easy stenciling project. Design variation: use these railing inspirations for making wall art.

[Tutorial](https://goo.gl/pJwEmL)

## 3. Skyline Necklace

![Skyline Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/09/33-560x420_560x420.jpg)

This is probably my favorite from this list of **NYC-based DIYs**. Isn't it fabulous? To make this, you will need copper wire, chain, pliers, and other jewelry components. What you do is form the wire into a recognizable NYC skyline and then add chains to both ends. Wear and enjoy!

[Tutorial](http://detrasdellabial.blogspot.com/2012/02/diy-new-york-necklace.html)

## 4. New York, New York Art

![New York, New York Art](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/09/42-560x373_560x373.jpg)

This is an adorable silhouette wall art inspired by New York brownstones. With a large canvas, spray paint, a Sharpie, and craft paint, you can easily make something similar for your home.

[Tutorial](http://thegainesgang4.blogspot.com/2011/04/nyc-canvas-art.html)

## 5. NYC Subway Dresser

![NYC Subway Dresser](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/09/53-560x410_560x410.jpg)

This is probably the most complicated of these **NYC-inspired DIYs**. But don't let that deter you, trying this project will result in a furniture that you can be proud of. The great thing about this idea is that you can apply it on other home projects, such as a table top, a headboard, and others.

[Tutorial](https://www.lindauerdesigns.com/how-to-create-your-own-nyc-subway-furniture-design/)

## 6. Skyline Shade

![Skyline Shade](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/09/63-560x468_560x468.jpg)

Isn't this pretty? I love that the bulb looks like the moon and the other holes make up the starry sky. You can try this method with an existing cylindrical lamp shade or construct one yourself. Check out the link for the video tutorial

[Tutorial](https://fashion-design.wonderhowto.com/how-to/make-punctured-paper-lamp-shade-with-threadbanger-351255/)

## 7. Drawer Accents

_ The part that NYC plays in this craft project is minimal but visually essential. What Steph did was hack several Ikea furniture pieces. He then added vintage NYC street maps as beautiful detailing for the drawer pulls and handles.

Inspiration_

## 8. Subway Sign Art

![Subway Sign Art](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/09/83_550x439.jpg)

Looking for an easy art project? Print subways signs on a computer and then Mod Podge them to pre-painted wood or canvas. Easy!

[Tutorial](https://www.bhg.com/bathroom/small/budget-small-baths/)

## 9. Taped Skyline

_ There are many NYC skyline decals sold today. If you're in a DIY mood, though, take out black tape and a straight edge and let your creativity flow. I bet your finished wall piece will not look any different from pricey decals.
