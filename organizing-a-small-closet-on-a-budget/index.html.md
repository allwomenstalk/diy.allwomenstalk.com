# Organizing a Small Closet on a Budget ...

I learned three new things about organizing from this video! My closet is small. I live on a budget! Even if your closet is big or you do not live on a budget, maybe this YouTube video can inspire you to make your closet neater.

My favorites are:

## 1. Containers

I absolutely loved the storage containers she uses for her clothes. They are not plastic like most containers are.

## 2. Roll

Once she had the storage containers, I really like how she "rolls" her clothes up. I usually either hang everything up or fold and place in a dresser drawer.

## 3. Accessories

With the accessory hanger, do you realize how much more room you can have by using the accessory hanger for your leggings?

A really cute video. Have you been inspired?

Thank you for watching!
