# Tidying up with Marie Kondo ...

The video goes quickly, so you might need to watch a few times. As I have noticed the craze over Marie Kondo and did not know who she was, I thought I would find out. In this video, **learn some organization tips from Marie Kondo**.

Place everything in a pile. Organize by size, vertically fold clothes in drawers, and place in cabinets by what you use the most.

Everything has to give you sparks of joy in order to keep it. No sparks of joy? Toss.

Pray or meditate, thanking your house for placing a roof over your head.

The little lady that she is knows how to hustle. When she sets out to do something, she does it. She is well-worth getting to know, whether by watching more YouTube videos about her, watching her on Netflix, or searching on Google.

Good luck!
