Frequently Asked Questions

What can I make with buttons?
You can make lots of crafts with buttons, like jewelry, wall art, decorated picture frames, pillows with button designs, and lots more. Get creative and use buttons to add fun details to almost any project!
Do I need special tools to make crafts with buttons?
Most of the time, you just need basic craft supplies like glue, scissors, and thread. Sometimes you might need special glue if you're sticking buttons to something like plastic or wood.
Can I use any buttons for my crafts?
Yes, you can use all sorts of buttons. Old ones from clothes you don't wear anymore, or new ones you buy from a craft store. Different sizes, colors, and shapes can make your craft more interesting.
What kind of glue should I use for sticking buttons to fabric?
Fabric glue is the best for sticking buttons to cloth. It's designed to hold things on fabric and usually can be washed without the buttons falling off.
Is crafting with buttons suitable for kids?
Absolutely! It's a fun way to help kids learn to be creative and practice their fine motor skills. Just watch little ones around small buttons, as they can be a choking hazard.
