# DIY Christmas Gift Mugs on the Cheap ...

Ok all you festive frugal ladies out there. I have another Christmas gift suggestion that can be given to anybody on your list, without emptying your wallet!

A little cappuccino with a little chocolate is sure to please and be appreciated on a freezing winter Christmas morning.

Personally, I would rather receive cappuccino instead of hot cocoa. How about you? Think of what you would rather receive before making these. Often, we give presents that we ourselves would not want. Think to yourself, "Would I want this present?"

Check your Sunday newspaper circulars and Coupons.com for coupons before going shopping. By doing so AND going to the dollar store, you can save mucho AND give something useful.

Under $3? Heck yeah.

Have a Merry Christmas!
