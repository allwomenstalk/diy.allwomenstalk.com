# 12 Insanely Adorable DIY Sock Toys ...

'Tis the season for snuggles so we give you here the most adorable DIY sock toys to cuddle with. Because aside from cuddling with cute toys, we also love recycling projects. So take out those socks you've never worn or the forlorn socks in the laundry room with no partners and whip up some of these cute **DIY sock creatures**. Happy sewing!

## 1. Dog

![Dog](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2013/01/13_450x320.jpg)

Aww, look at him! He's even making cute puppy eyes. To make this little cutie, you will be needing only one sock. You will also need buttons, stuffing, felt, and fleece fabric. Check out Cut Out and Keep for the complete tutorial.

[Tutorial](https://www.cutoutandkeep.net/projects/single-sock-dog-tutorial)

## 2. Monkey

![Monkey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2013/01/23_588x480.jpg)

These are bursting with cute, too. Unlike the first **DIY sock toy**, you will be needing a pair of crew length socks to make one monkey. From the blog: Since the sock is stretched after stuffed, thick sock yields better quality than thin sock.

[Tutorial](https://www.craftpassion.com/how-to-sew-sock-monkey/)

## 3. Cow

![Cow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2013/01/32_640x480.jpg)

Not only do I love the pink, gray, and white colors of these adorable cows, I love the way the cuffs were utilized as feet, too. Of course, the general cuteness is also a good thing.

[Tutorial](http://craft-with-confidence.blogspot.com/2010/12/sock-cow-tutorial.html)

## 4. Bunny

![Bunny](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2013/01/43_502x329.jpg)

These **sock bunny tutorial** is brought to us by the talented Ashley of Lil Blue Boo. Ashley shares: All you need is some nice knee high socks and some simple sewing supplies. I sewed mine on a machine but they can be easily hand stitched too! I am on a hand sewing kick lately so I might give these a go.

[Tutorial](https://www.lilblueboo.com/2012/03/a-sock-bunny-a-tutorial.html)

## 5. Snow Dude

![Snow Dude](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2013/01/53_400x265.jpg)

Sure Christmas is over but it might still be snowing in your part of the world. If you want to make a snowman but you don't relish the thought of stepping out, white socks are your best friends.

[Tutorial](http://thepurplepug.blogspot.com/2011/12/sock-snow-dude-tutorial.html)

## 6. Kitty

![Kitty](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2013/01/63_450x320.jpg)

This is my favorite from this list of** DIY sock toys**. Because I am a cat person and also because this smiling face is irresistible.

[Tutorial](https://www.craftpassion.com/sewing-sock-kitty-tutorial-guest-tutor/)

## 7. Hobby Horse

![Hobby Horse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2013/01/73_500x333.jpg)

Okay, this particular **DIY sock toy** may not exactly be great for cuddles but I had to include it here. This craft project is simply too awesome not to be a part of this list.

[Tutorial](http://radmegan.blogspot.com/2012/02/diy-sock-hobby-horses.html)

## 8. Octopus

![Octopus](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2013/01/83_640x440.jpg)

Your kids love octopus? I bet they do; these sea creatures are simply too clever and cute not to be appreciated. To make your very own land-dwelling socktopus, you will be needing one pair of socks, stuffing, needle and thread, and a sewing machine.

[Tutorial](https://goo.gl/Gsp1Cg)

## 9. Bunny II

![Bunny II](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2013/01/9a_588x392.jpg)

Unlike the first sock bunny DIY I posted here, this craft project will give you a more traditional rabbit plushie look. The finished toys are a bit small, too, making them perfect as hangings or parts of a mobile.

[Tutorial](https://www.craftpassion.com/sock-bunny-sewing-tutorial/)

## 10. Totoro

![Totoro](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2013/01/101_800x569.jpg)

How cute! Love My Neighbor Totoro? If you do and you can't find the original plushies sold by Studio Ghibli, you can make a few of these sock toys.

[Tutorial](https://www.cutoutandkeep.net/projects/totoro)

## 11. Sheep

![Sheep](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2013/01/111_450x338.jpg)

Eep, too cute! The "naked" body of this cutie is made of a sock. For the wool, you will be needing white fleece. Check out the DIY for the complete how-to.

[Tutorial](http://www.treegoldandbeegold.com/2012/02/tutorial-sweetheart-sheep-plush-sock.html)

## 12. Owl

![Owl](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2013/01/121_450x320.jpg)

We cannot make a list of sock toys without including a pattern for owls. Owls are the best.

[Tutorial](https://www.favecrafts.com/Sewing/Owl-Sock-Friends#?ss=85386&cs=15575_1658525223_26845bd4f7761614be61e5762a107452)

If you think that these **DIY sock toys** are too small for a quality snuggle fest, I suggest that you try to make all toys. That way, you'll have a platoon of** DIY sock toys** to keep you warm.
