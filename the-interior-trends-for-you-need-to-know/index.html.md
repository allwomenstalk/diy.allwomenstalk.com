# The Interior Trends for 2019 You Need to Know ...

It would be fair to say that with each new year, there come new trends in all areas of life from music to fashion to food to beauty, and interior design and décor is definitely no exception! If you are thinking about giving your home a big makeover in the next twelve months, then you need to make sure that you aren’t going to pick designs and colours that are going to be seen as old hat and ugly this time next year! Here are some of the biggest and best **interiors trends for 2019** you need to know.

## 1. Mixing Metals

![furniture, table, floor, lamp, interior design,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kb/sp/zeesbrnf5bfe75968849c685280107_600x600.jpg)

2019 isn’t a year to pick one type of metal fixing and stick with it. The trend in 2019 is all about mixing metals to create depth and character for the room. Putting things like brass, gold and copper together can create a really luxurious yet bohemian look, feeling both warm and sophisticated at the same time.

## 2. Home Bar

![interior design, furniture, table, product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wh/uk/qr031d1e5bfe77fc20579166096081_600x600.jpg)

Why go out to the pub when you can have your own set up at home? Home bars are going to be all the rage, and I’m not talking about a full panelled room, more of a portable shelf system or dedicated unit to hold your liquor and accessories, that has pride of place in a shared space.

## 3. Jewel Tones

![living room, room, home, couch, furniture,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lv/x0/nllbxqf65bfe76d3c22ea217102548_600x600.jpg)

Jewel colours are going to be all the rage in 2019. They can help you to make a real statement, especially when using them in a room with predominantly neutral colours in order to soften them and make them less harsh and out of place.

## 4. Velvet

![living room, furniture, couch, floor, flooring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ct/fo/wpdkykji5bfe7651608a2875782293_600x600.jpg)

Nothing says luxurious living more than velvet, and in 2019 more and more of us are going to be going in that direction. It’s a great fabric for beds, chairs, cushions, and sofas, instantly turning any mundane piece of furniture into a work of comfortable art!

## 5. Florals

![wall, living room, interior design, flower, couch,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oe/ol/snmnenu45bfe7781bb186250848312_600x520.jpg)

Florals come in and out of style, and 2019 is set to be very much an ‘in’ year. Floral themed statement walls are going to be on a lot of people’s walls, along with a rise in artwork for the home that is going to be very floral themed.

## 6. Spa Inspiration

![architecture, bathroom, interior design, glass, door,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l2/k7/xtbvr8435bfe75dc4be99374203355_600x600.jpg)

Lots of bathrooms are going to be styled with a classic spa vibe in 2019. Rather than filling your washroom with all the latest gadgets and fluffy mats, the trend is going to be for more spacious, minimalist design that incorporates only the few high quality pieces that you need in a bathroom.
