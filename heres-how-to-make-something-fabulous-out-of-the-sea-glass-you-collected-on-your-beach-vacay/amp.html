1 How to Drill Sea Glass for a Pendant

Source: Sea Glass Jewelry by Ecstasea

***

Making a pendant out of sea glass is a great way to turn your beach vacation memories into something special. It’s a fun and creative DIY project that’s sure to make your friends and family jealous.

The first step is to find some sea glass. If you haven’t been to the beach lately, you can purchase sea glass online or at craft stores. Once you have your sea glass, you’ll need to clean it. This can be done with a soft brush and some warm soapy water.

The next step is to drill a hole in the sea glass. This is best done with a diamond drill bit, which can be purchased at most hardware stores. It’s important to use the correct drill bit, as sea glass is very hard and can easily break if the wrong bit is used.

Once the hole is drilled, you’ll need to attach a bail or jump ring to the sea glass. This is the metal loop that will hold the pendant in place. You can find these at craft stores or online.

7 Make This Easy Sea Glass Bowl with Glue and Plastic Sandwich Wrap

31 Make This Sea Glass Mosaic Table Top

Source: Sea Glass Mobile and Mosaic

***

Transforming your gathered sea glass into a stunning mosaic table top is an enchanting way to preserve your beach memories. Begin by selecting a simple table with a flat surface. Clean your sea glass and sort by color and size to create a harmonious design. Use clear adhesive to secure each piece of glass, starting from the center and working outwards. Once your pattern is complete, apply grout to fill the spaces in between, wiping away any excess. Allow it to dry, then seal your mosaic for a durable and luminous finish. The end result will be both a functional furniture piece and a personalized work of art.
