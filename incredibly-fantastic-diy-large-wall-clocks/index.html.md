# 9 Incredibly Fantastic DIY Large Wall Clocks ...

So you got a blank wall in your home and you're thinking of giving it a DIY giant clock treatment. Your search for great ideas ends here. The following are amazing DIY ideas for making your very own large wall clock. Some of these are simple recycling projects. Others require more complicated steps but they are all oh-so-fantastic. Hope you like them!

## 1. Photo

![Photo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p3/x6/l9un980b54cd9b1553b0a162588188_600x600.jpg)

A photo wall and a DIY giant clock in one? This is one fun project, I tell you. The clock movement used for this project is a type with a self-contained housing. They can be mounted to the wall directly, unlike others that need to have a base.

Source: [myhomemystyle.com](http://www.myhomemystyle.com/enotes/2013/07/26/make-an-oversized-photo-wall-clock/)

## 2. Pocket Watch

_ This is my favorite from this list of large wall clock projects. This beautifully whimsical clock was made using a standard wall clock covered with pretty gold leaf. If you don't want to go through the leafing process, spraying the clock frame and plastic chain with gold paint will work, too.

Source: designsponge.com_

## 3. Rustic Metal

![Rustic Metal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l6/wi/cz4vddrc54cd9b1768bf1317996662_600x800.jpg)

This working large wall clock has a beautiful rustic style. The back is made of wood with support bands underneath. The numbers and inner circle details are made of metal. The frame is PVC lattice but when spray painted the same color as the numbers, a lovely faux metal look was achieved.

Source: [myrepurposedlife.com](https://www.myrepurposedlife.com/large-rustic-clock)

## 4. Stenciled Pallet

![Stenciled Pallet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n7/mi/lsl679b654cd9b163b54b292393468_600x534.jpg)

Pallets are great for constructing the base of your oversized wall clock. This particular DIY tutorial will show you how to assemble said clock base. It's also a fantastic resource for tips on how to stencil a design to your giant wall clock.

Source: [flamingotoes.com](https://flamingotoes.com/large-stencilled-pallet-clock-tutorial/)

## 5. Rectangular and Rustic

![Rectangular and Rustic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f1/ze/a1i1zj4w54cd9b175794f916418579_600x913.jpg)

Not all large wall clocks are round. This rectangular one is made of rustic wood finished with a bit of paint and some wood stain. If you want a bit of color, it is also possible to finish this with some lovely paint.

Source: [cherishedbliss.com](https://cherishedbliss.com/rustic-wooden-clock-tutorial/)

## 6. Upcycled round Table

![Upcycled round Table](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mr/zg/uc8ijkaa54cd9b159b634524987736_400x600.jpg)

You don't always have to work with raw pieces of wood to come up with a giant wall clock base. A table top could work, too. Using chalkboard paint as finishing will look great for a trendy large wall clock as well.

Source: [upcycledtreasures.com](https://mountainmodernlife.com/)

## 7. Pallet Clock

![Pallet Clock](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q6/zv/ttikjg9154cd9b17ace6c700757865_600x846.jpg)

You know by now that pallets are fantastic for making giant wall clocks. But while the previous DIY ideas required taking the pallet apart, that isn't the only way to go about using this material. Sometimes, leaving the pallet as it is will work as well. You simply clean the pallet up, add the clock parts, and hang.

Source: [sugarbeecrafts.com](https://goo.gl/z0qxUY)

## 8. Giant Tower Clock

![Giant Tower Clock](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x0/xg/dxo5fg6n54cd9b16cfbfc134579548_441x640.jpg)

This beautiful giant wall clock is only decorative but it sure packs a punch. The finished piece has a circumference of about 48 inches. It was made with a piece of plywood but if you want something much bigger, two pieces of plywood put together will look nice as well. The numbers here were painted and the raised grid is made of fiberboard.

Source: [epbot.com](https://www.epbot.com/2013/09/diy-giant-tower-wall-clock.html)

## 9. Metallic Style

![Metallic Style](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/be/v8/jklv37vo54cd9b18493de971816400_600x700.jpg)

This is one pretty DIY giant wall clock as well. It has that vintage metallic look but it's actually made of a wooden round table top. It was spray painted two shades of bronze to achieve this fun metallic look.

Source: [diyready.com](https://diyprojects.com/make-giant-wall-clock-decorative)

How gorgeous are these DIY giant wall clocks? If you don't want the idea of listening to a giant clock tick-tocking in your home, the faux ones are your best friends. Happy making!
