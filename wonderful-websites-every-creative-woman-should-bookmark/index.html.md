# Creative Girls Need These Killer Websites in Their Lives ...

If you're passionate about your art, you should visit websites for creative minds. You don't want your talents to go to waste, after all. That's why you should bookmark a few websites that are meant to motivate you. Here are a few wonderful websites every creative woman should check out as soon as possible:

## 1. Custom IPhone Case

There's no reason to buy an already made iPhone case when you can create your own on [custom.case-mate.com](https://goo.gl/EKsHXO). This site allows you to design your own case by adding text, pictures, and colors to it. You can either add photographs that you've found online or snapshots that you've taken on your very own phone. It's all up to you, so make it as awesome as you can!

## 2. Ted

You don't need to have Netflix to watch those famous Ted talks you're always hearing about. Head to [ted.com](https://www.ted.com/) to watch videos, read articles, and even participate in your favorite Ted Talks. It's a great site to visit if you want to learn about the world around you. A great deal of them are given by creative minds, so they just might give you the motivation you need.

## 3. Psyko Paint

Love to mess around with photos? On [paint.psykopaint.com](https://goo.gl/zaGdjT) you can upload a photograph and then alter it in any way you please. There are some pretty cool things you can do with the brushes they give you that you won't come across on any other website. If you're in the mood to be creative, then this site can waste a few hours of your time. It's pretty fun once you get used to it.

## 4. Deviant Art

If you're an artist, you'll love [deviantart.com](https://www.deviantart.com/). It allows people of any age to show off their artwork. Of course, there's also a section for writers. If you want to show off your fan art, fan fiction, or even original pieces, then this is a great place for you to get exposure.

## 5. Picasso Head

If you have nothing to do and want to waste your time with a mindless, artsy game, head to _picassohead.com_. All you have to do is click a few buttons to make your own Picasso styled masterpiece. It won't be anything special, but it just might give you inspiration to create your own artwork. You never know.

## 6. Sound Matrix Game

[Buzzedgames.com](https://goo.gl/krG9k6) is a fun little game that allows you to make music. When you're bored and have nothing better to do, it's a great way to waste time. If you're a composer, hearing a few random notes could inspire you, so you might as well play around with the game. It certainly can't hurt.

## 7. Etsy

Pretty much everyone has heard of [etsy.com](https://www.etsy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com), because it's a great place to buy and sell handmade products. If you're looking for a super specific fandom piece, then Etsy probably has it. If you're looking to make some extra cash, then you can create your own account and start selling your crafts. It's a great way to show off your skills while making money.

Don't let your creativity go to waste. Show off your skills whenever you can, because you deserve recognition--or at least the pride of knowing you did a job well done. What other creative websites do you love to visit?
