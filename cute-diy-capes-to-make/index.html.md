# 10 Cute DIY Capes to Make ...

I read somewhere that capes will become trendy soon and what better way to stay fashionable than making your own DIY Cape, right? We have 10 **DIY capes** here that you will want to have a go together with your trusty sewing machine. Eight of these** cape tutorials** are for adults and two are for children. The two are simply too adorable for me to ignore so I gave them a place in this list of **DIY capes.** Hope you like them!

## 1. Fleece Blanket

![Fleece Blanket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/11/19_450x320.jpg)

This chic **DIY cape** used to be a fleece blanket. Awesome, right? Aside from the cozy blanket, you will also need buttons, scissors, and a sewing machine to make this beautiful outerwear. If you don't have a sewing machine, you can easily hand sew this project.

[Tutorial](https://www.favecrafts.com/Wearable-Crafts/DIY-Fleece-Cape/ml/1?ss=85386&cs=15575_1658929983_b7ec36d3535b3354819a0b9a948e239a)

## 2. Rain Cape

![Rain Cape](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/11/28-560x420_560x420.jpg)

This tutorial is brought to us by the very talented and stylish A Pair & A Spare. This easy DIY was inspired by transparent rain capes from Burberry. To make this, you simply make a circular pattern using plastic sheeting, embellish the hem and front opening with patterned duct tape, sew ribbon around the neckline, and add leather buckle to the front.

[Tutorial](http://apair-andaspare.blogspot.com/2012/07/diy-burberry-inspired-transparent-rain.html)

## 3. Camel Blanket

![Camel Blanket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/11/37-560x368_560x368.jpg)

Here we have another **DIY cape** made of a blanket. It was made in a color popular many seasons ago but you can easily make it trendy by using maroon or oxblood fabric.

[Tutorial](https://goo.gl/9g6J3J)

## 4. Multi-Style and Reversible

![Multi-Style and Reversible](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/11/48_400x296.jpg)

Don't you just love DIY pieces of clothing that you can style in many ways and also happen to be reversible? This cape is one perfect example. The best part about this tutorial is that it looks very easy to do. Check out the link for the PDF pattern.

[Tutorial](http://www.liaspace.com/2010/12/tutorial-multi-reversible-cape.html)

## 6. Belted

Tutorial (in German)_

## 7. Houndstooth

![Houndstooth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/11/78_450x320.jpg)

How chic is the fabric pattern of this DIY cape? Houndstooth will never go out of style so you might want to consider that if you want to sew your own cape, too. For this particular pattern, choosing a contrasting color for the lining will give you one stylish piece of outerwear.

[Tutorial](http://matterofstyle.blogspot.com/2012/02/cape-coat-diy-free-pattern.html)

## 8. Embellished

_ This is another **DIY cape idea** by PS - I Made This. Unlike the camel piece, this particular cape is made of silk chiffon and embellished with sequins. This is perfect for keeping you warm during an outdoor autumn formal party. Of course, you can also use this cape indoors.

Tutorial_

## 9. Red Riding Hood

![Red Riding Hood](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/11/96_450x320.jpg)

Okay, this might not be a conventional modern day outerwear but really, there is no one stopping you from rocking it. If you construct this in a color other than red, it will be a great coat/cape for keeping you warm.
