# 7 Stenciled Pillow Cases That Are Too Adorable for Words ...

Stenciled pillow cases add a touch of charm to any living room. Aside from making your home look extra cozy, they're also great because they are easy and fun projects. Using stencils that you can make yourself or ready-made designs, stenciled pillow case possibilities are endless. Let's check out some inspiration below:

## 1. Arrow

![Arrow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/18232020_3712_600x400.jpg)

If you want a stenciled pillow case that is on trend, this arrow idea is right up your alley. To make your version, you can choose to draw the arrows directly on the freezer paper. You can also print your design if you're not so sure about your arrow-drawing skills.

**Tutorial Source:** [makinghomebase.com](https://www.makinghomebase.com/diy-stenciled-arrow-pillow/)

## 2. Wordy

![Wordy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/18232020_2900_545x395.jpg)

This is a fun home décor DIY project that you can do using those plastic alphabet stencils. You can get them in any school supply store. They are also sold in toy and craft stores. What's great about using this material is that they come in many designs. Some letters are thick while others are of the slim type. Another reason why this DIY stenciling project is great is the fact that you can write a sweet message on the pillow. Great gift idea, right?

**Tutorial Source:** [redflycreations.com](http://www.redflycreations.com/2012/02/stencil-pillow.html)

## 3. Repeat Print

![Repeat Print](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/18232020_6342_540x429.jpg)

This gorgeous stenciled pillow case looks store-bought, doesn't it? This is only a DIY, though, and you can make your own version, too. Instead of freezer paper, Miriam suggests using a proper stenciling film. This way, you can use the pattern again, especially if it turns out fabulous. She also gives us great tips on what type of knife and adhesive spray to use.

**Tutorial Source:** [madmim.com](http://survey-smiles.com/)

## 4. Monogram and House Number

![Monogram and House Number](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/18232020_2040_400x267.jpg)

Here is one fun stenciled pillow case project that you can display outside your home. These can be artfully propped on a porch chair. If you live in an apartment building, you can choose to make the monogram pillow case instead. These were created using handy-dandy freezer paper.

**Tutorial Source:** [nashvillepug.blogspot.com](http://nashvillepug.blogspot.com/2011/12/stenciled-burlap-pillow-covers.html?m=1)

## 5. Multiple Design

![Multiple Design](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/18232022_5081_600x398.jpg)

Isn't this pretty? If you're feeling extra crafty and quite uninhibited, I suggest going the multiple template route when making your DIY stenciled pillow case. The damask pattern used here was a ready-made design. The bird, however, was a stencil made using a greeting card. Clever!

**Tutorial Source:** [jenniferrizzo.com](https://jenniferrizzo.com/stenciled-linen-pillows/)

## 6. Coral

![Coral](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/18232022_4375_500x428.jpg)

This is a wonderful inspiration if you want a nature-inspired stencil for your pillow case. If you find making stencils with straight lines harder than one with curves, this coral idea should be great. With a few tweaks, this design can look like a tree, too.

**Tutorial Source:** [mermag.blogspot.com](http://mermag.blogspot.com/2011/08/diy-coral-motif-pillow.html)

## 7. Neon Pop

![Neon Pop](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/18232023_0734_600x397.jpg)

This beautiful home décor project shows you a way to add a playful touch to your stenciled pillow case. For most of your design, choose a paint color that is only a few shades darker than the textile of the pillow case. For visual interest, stencil neon elements here and there. This idea will work wonderfully if your stencil is comprised of small designs.

**Tutorial Source:** [lebenslustiger.com](http://www.lebenslustiger.com/serendipity/archives/101-Hop-Along-Neon-Bunny.html)

I hope you found fabulous stenciled pillow case inspirations from this list. Try one or two of these designs or templates this week. If you enjoyed what you did, include these projects in your handmade gift ideas for Christmas. Any recipient of a stenciled pillow case would consider herself lucky. Don't forget to let us hear about some of your design as well. Happy stenciling!
