# The Best Way to Get Organized According to Your Zodiac Sign ...

Want to know the **best way to get organized, according to your zodiac sign**? As human beings, it might be fair to say that we spend a considerable amount of time in our lives trying to get everything organized! The feeling of having sorted through a bunch of problems, both physical and psychological, in order to achieve positive clarity, is something that we all strive to do, but the tricky thing about doing this is that there are so many different approaches to organization that it can often be hard to find the one that suits you best. If you are looking to get organized yourself, then why not turn to the stars for inspiration? Here is the **best way to get organized, according to your zodiac sign**.

## 1. Aries

You have a tendency to be so busy that you forget the most important things, so a good tip for you would be to put up a pegboard where you can keep all of the essentials right there in sight and on hand.

## 2. Taurus

Your main area of organisation is always the kitchen, so why not make finding ingredients extra easy by pimping out your store cupboard with a set of clear glass mason jars. Easy sight and access to everything.

## 3. Gemini

You need some structure as soon as you walk through the front door, which makes a shoe and coat rack the perfect thing to get you started!

## 4. Cancer

Family is everything to you, so something that you can do to organize yourself and indulge that side of you is to finally get round to making those photo albums and scrapbooks of your family pics.

## 5. Leo

You pride yourself on having a dramatic impact, so your home should too! Why not try to organise your bookshelves by colour? It is a really beautiful and quirky way to decorate.

## 6. Virgo

You feel calmest when in touch with nature, so spending some time in the garden, getting it looking in fine shape for summer parties and BBQs would be a good job for you.

## 7. Libra

True Libras have way too many garments to deal with, so you need to start with your closet, organising things by season and by colour. And be strict with yourself - anything that you haven’t worn for years goes to charity!

## 8. Scorpio

You know how a Libra is with clothes? Well, you are like that with pretty much everything! The only way to get truly organised for a Scorpio is to turn the house completely inside out. Be ruthless, label boxes with ‘keep’, ‘trash’ and ‘charity’ and make sure they are all filled by the end of the day!

## 9. Sagittarius

You thrive when out in nature, so why not set up a garden shed that you can use as a little outdoor activities space?

## 10. Capricorn

You are extremely hardworking, and to achieve your best potential you need to have a workstation that is spotless.

## 11. Aquarius

You are a humanitarian by nature, so the first thing you need to get organised is the recycling element of your home.

## 12. Pisces

You are a true homebody, so the first place that comes to mind when trying to organise should be your linen closet! Make sure everything is clean, fresh, and ready to go when needed.
