# 17 Amazing Things You Can Make out of Pallets ...

Pallets are the perfect solution whether you own or rent, whether you have tons of space or just a little, whether you're handy and artistic or the exact opposite. They're perfect for interior and exterior design, plus you can build just about anything you might need.

## 1. An Outdoor Bar

![An Outdoor Bar](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hp/iq/vjtn6sne569469aab1994450786895_600x800.jpg)

Source: [lovethehereandnow.com](http://www.lovethehereandnow.com/cgi-sys/suspendedpage.cgi)

A painted pallet, a top made of, oh, anything you like, and you're done. Though, you could add casters if you like, and make it mobile. Make it wider, build it longer – endless possibilities, pals.

## 2. And the Ultimate Chill-out Spot to Go with It

![And the Ultimate Chill-out Spot to Go with It](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n5/ey/nn1eehe75694688c8fa2f238009492_600x557.jpg)

Source: [frecklesandfluff.com](http://www.frecklesandfluff.com/home/timber-pallet-chillout-lounge)

Seriously, if you don't have a lot of money or space for outdoor seating, this is so easy and cheap to make! Plus, of course, you can basically customize something like this any way you want.

## 3. How about a Swing Bed?

![How about a Swing Bed?](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bk/ci/cvtkfclr569468e5c19ac014542545_500x751.jpg)

Source: [themerrythought.com](https://themerrythought.com/diy/diy-pallet-swing-bed/)

Swings are the best things, but a swing that doubles as a bed? That's even better. Imagine summer days spent stretched out on your porch or under your favorite tree!

## 4. Try an Indoor Wine Bar

![Try an Indoor Wine Bar](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eg/wn/hnsg4tm95694688d4ed74067395504_427x640.jpg)

![Speaking of Dining Tables](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zl/y8/p4ngkz4b569468903128a867659921_600x800.jpg)

## 8. Nooks for Your Books!

![Nooks for Your Books!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hs/bm/hpb13sa5569468910becb463595896_535x799.jpg)

## 13. An Effortless Herb Garden

![An Effortless Herb Garden](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qq/jw/r1ex69do5694689722acb758696502_600x803.jpg)
