# How to Declutter Your House and Have Fun While Doing It ...

If you are able to move your body and to count to 27, you can do this!

Ms. Dame read the book "Sink Reflections" and got her inspiration to declutter.

Like the video teaches, turn on the music and go about your house, each time, counting 27 items to get rid of. That's it!

Most often, you are going to end up with even more than 27 items, and that is OK!

What to do with these things? Donate to GoodWill, Salvation Army, etc. One person's trash is another person's treasure.
