22 HOW to Tie Ribbon like a Pro

Via HOW TO tie ribbon like ...

Add some sophistication and flair to your gift wrapping.

***

For a professional-looking ribbon bow, start by cutting a length of ribbon about four times the size of the desired finished bow. Tie the ribbon in an overhand knot at the center, then fan out the loops to the desired size. Twist the loops together and secure with a second knot. For extra flair, add two more loops to the bow by making a second overhand knot in the center. Finally, trim the ends of the ribbon to the desired length. With this simple technique, you can easily create beautiful, professional-looking ribbon bows for any gift wrapping occasion.
