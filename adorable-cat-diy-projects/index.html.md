# 10 Adorable Cat DIY Projects ...

I just lamented over at [my blog](https://www.diyncrafts.com/) about the lack of cute cat print stuff in my city so I set about finding Cat-inspired DIY Projects. I actually found a fun way to make cat prints from this list of** feline-themed crafts**. While I prepare for the materials for that particular project, I give you these very adorable **cat DIY ideas**. Enjoy!

## 1. Black Cat Pillows

![Black Cat Pillows](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/09/110_300x214.jpg)

These little fellas make me giggle. They're Halloween decor but they look like they've eaten two buckets worth of candy. To make this, you will need crystal buttons and two strands of floss. This is a simple hand sewing project that will not take more than an hour.

[Tutorial](https://www.womansday.com/home/crafts-projects/how-to/a3063/halloween-crafts-black-cat-pillows-21977/)

## 2. Cat Skirt

[img.allw.mn](https://img.allw.mn/content/diy/2012/09/2_525x437.png)

Yes, that is a cat skirt. If you're into wearing clothing that screams ADORABLE, making this** cat DIY** should be part of your to-do list. You will need a black skirt, black felt, buttons (pink and white), and embroidery thread for this project.

[Tutorial](http://scathingly-brilliant.blogspot.com/2011/11/diy-cat-skirt.html)

## 3. Cat Shirt

![Cat Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/09/38-560x417_560x417.jpg)

This is the **cat DIY idea** that I will be copying. Isn't it neat? Materials needed for this craft project are plain shirt, sponge, fabric paint, and paintbrush. You simply cut the sponge to the shape of a cat face, dab paint on it, and print on your shirt. Let dry and style.

[Tutorial](https://goo.gl/EsVVHq)

## 7. Cat Backpack

![Cat Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/09/76-560x438_560x438.jpg)

Okay, this is a tad complicated compared to the other **cat tutorials** here but it's just too cute not to be included. Perhaps when you're feeling quite ambitious and you want a sewing project that is both cute and functional, you will want to look into this backpack tutorial.

## 8. Keyboard Cat

![Keyboard Cat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/09/87_480x350.jpg)

I need this. Even though, sometimes, I have real cats resting right in front of my keyboard. Design variation: use black fabric or even stripe-y orange/gray fabric to mimic a tabby cat.

## 10. Cat Brooch
