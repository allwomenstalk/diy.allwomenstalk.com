# How to Make Shaving Cream Art ...

This artwork is not typical. Give it a little style for your woman cave! Learn **how to make shaving cream art** with this video!

You need a square pizza tray, some shaving cream, some Q-tips, some parchment paper and a decoration, such as a monogram as shown in the video.

This seems really unique. Either create for your own decoration, give as a present, or even maybe think of it as a side hustle to make some spare cash. Also, maybe use as wrapping paper!
