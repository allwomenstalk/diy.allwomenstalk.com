# 4 Organizing Tips for Your Tidiest Home Yet ...

Do you feel ever like however much organization you do, it is never enough? No matter how much I do, it is never enough.

After watching this video, I have learned a lesson from ClutterBug's tips. Ready to get organized?

ClutterBug
Published on Jul 25, 2018

I am totally sold on her baskets and bins. I do this already with a few items, but I would rather have covers and labels like ClutterBug's. I don't want to have to dig through everything to find what I am looking for!

Next, I am totally sold on purging from the closet and already do this every-so-often. When I go through my closet to eliminate, I donate the articles of clothing. However, be certain that the clothes are gently used, not stained or full of holes. Call me old-fashioned, I know the style is holes in the pants, but you know what I mean!

Following, I like her 10 minutes tidy before bed. I like to have the dishes washed before I am out for the night. It may be something simple, but who wants to wake up to the Leaning Tower of Pisa in the kitchen sink?

These may be common sense but are cute reminders of what to do to make your home a sanctuary.

Thank you for watching!
