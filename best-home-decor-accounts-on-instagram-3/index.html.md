# 22 Brilliant Home Decor Accounts on Instagram for Serious Design Inspiration

Is your living space starting to feel a bit stale? Whether you're planning a full renovation or just looking for a weekend refresh, Instagram is a treasure trove of inspiration. We've curated a list of stunning home decor accounts that will help you transform your space into a personal sanctuary. From minimalist vibes to cozy maximalism, these creators offer endless **decor inspos from Instagram** to spark your next project.

Before you dive into these accounts, you might also want to check out our guide on [simple ways to refresh your home decor on a budget](https://diy.allwomenstalk.com/simple-ways-to-refresh-your-home-decor-on-a-budget/) for even more practical tips.

## 1. @homedeconl

If you love clean lines and a touch of Dutch design, [@homedeconl](https://www.instagram.com/homedeconl/) is a must-follow. They specialize in showcasing modern interiors that feel both sophisticated and livable.

## 2. @apartmenttherapy

[Apartment Therapy](https://www.apartmenttherapy.com/) is the ultimate resource for anyone living in small spaces. Their Instagram feed is packed with real-life solutions and DIY projects that prove you don't need a mansion to have a beautiful home.

## 3. @innerdecor

[@innerdecor](https://www.instagram.com/innerdecor/) focuses on the finer details that make a room pop. From unique textures to bold color palettes, their posts are perfect for those who want to add a unique flair to their interiors.

## 4. @homepolish

[Homepolish](https://www.homepolish.com/) represents the pinnacle of interior design services, offering a glimpse into professionally curated spaces that are as functional as they are beautiful.

## 5. @leannefordinteriors

Leanne Ford is known for her iconic "warm minimalist" style. Following [@leannefordinteriors](https://www.instagram.com/leannefordinteriors/) will give you plenty of ideas on how to use white space and natural materials effectively.

## 6. @amberinteriors

[Amber Lewis](https://amberinteriordesign.com/) of Amber Interiors is a master of the California cool aesthetic. Her feed is a masterclass in layering patterns and vintage finds.

## 7. @katemarkerinteriors

For a look that is timeless and elegant, [Kate Marker](https://katemarkerinteriors.com/) provides endless inspiration. Her designs often feature classic elements with a fresh, modern twist.

## 8. @decoristofficial

[Decorist](https://www.decorist.com/) offers online interior design services, and their Instagram showcases the amazing transformations they've achieved for clients across all styles and budgets.

## 9. @the_real_houses_of_ig

This account is exactly what it sounds like—a peek into some of the most beautiful real-life homes on Instagram. It's a great place to see how different trends are being implemented in everyday living.

## 10. @studiomcgee

Shea McGee and her team at [Studio McGee](https://studio-mcgee.com/) have become household names for a reason. Their light-filled, airy designs are consistently top-tier.

## 11. @beckiowens

[Becki Owens](https://beckiowens.com/) is another staple for those who love the modern coastal or farmhouse aesthetic. Her feed is incredibly cohesive and inspiring.

## 12. @emilyhenderson

Emily Henderson is a stylist and New York Times best-selling author. Her Instagram [@emilyhenderson](https://www.instagram.com/emilyhenderson/) is full of personality, color, and expert styling tips.

## 13. @witanddelight_

[Wit & Delight](https://witanddelight.com/) is a lifestyle brand that focuses on designing a life well-lived. Their interior posts are thoughtful and deeply personal.

## 14. @thejungalow

If you love plants and bohemian vibes, Justina Blakeney's [Jungalow](https://www.jungalow.com/) is the place for you. It's an explosion of color and pattern that will make any space feel alive.

## 15. @graymalin

While primarily a photographer, [Gray Malin's](https://www.graymalin.com/) Instagram often features how his stunning aerial prints can transform a room's atmosphere.

## 16. @serenaandlily

[Serena & Lily](https://www.serenaandlily.com/) is the go-to for a high-end coastal look. Their feed is filled with beautiful linens, wicker textures, and oceanic hues.

## 17. @anthropologiehome

[Anthropologie Home](https://www.anthropologie.com/home-garden) is perfect for those who want a touch of whimsy and artistic flair in their decor. Their pieces are truly unique and full of character.

## 18. @westelm

For mid-century modern enthusiasts, [West Elm](https://www.westelm.com/) is a constant source of inspiration. Their Instagram shows how to style their iconic pieces in various settings.

## 19. @cb2

[CB2](https://www.cb2.com/) offers a more modern, urban take on home furnishings. Their feed is sleek, edgy, and always on-trend.

## 20. @potterybarn

[Pottery Barn](https://www.potterybarn.com/) is a classic for a reason. Their Instagram provides timeless decorating ideas that focus on comfort and high-quality craftsmanship.

## 21. @magnolia

Chip and Joanna Gaines' [Magnolia](https://magnolia.com/) brand redefined modern farmhouse style. Their feed continues to inspire millions with its focus on home, family, and beautiful design.

## 22. @dominomag

[Domino](https://domino.com/) is a creative guide to living with style. Their Instagram is a vibrant mix of home tours, DIY projects, and shopping guides.

To help you decide where to start your following spree, here's a quick comparison of some popular styles featured above:
