Ways to Bring Summer into Your Home for Less than $50 ...

Pineapple Glass Markers • Flamingo Candle • Backgammon &amp; Checkers Set • Rainbow Neon Light • Picnic Umbrella • More ...

By Neecey

There are some super cute ways to bring summer into your home. Summer is the season to brighten and lighten. It’s also a time when you can have a bit of fun with your accessories, simply because when the sun shines, everyone smiles just that bit more. I’ve picked out some ways to bring summer into your home with bright, bold and fun accessories. Enjoy!

1. Pineapple Glass Markers

One of the best parts of summer is inviting your friends round to have a party, and one cool way to bring the summer theme in is with this set of brightly colored glass markers to adorn your cocktails!

$11 at shop.nordstrom.com

2. Flamingo Candle

The summer trend for flamingo designs is still as strong this year as it was last year, and one great way of introducing the summer animal design in to your home is with this super candle. Not only great looking but also serving a function!

$35 at shop.nordstrom.com

3. Backgammon & Checkers Set

Lazy days at home during the summer are all about playing games with your family and friends, and this vibrantly designed backgammon and checkers set makes for a really fun addition to your home. Get practicing!

$21 at shop.nordstrom.com

4. Rainbow Neon Light

Summer should be all about fun, and this cute neon rainbow light can bring a lot of personality in to a room in your home with very little effort! Switch it on when the sun goes down and get the party started!

$26 at shop.nordstrom.com

5. Picnic Umbrella

Turn your garden into the beach with this stylish picnic umbrella, doing the job of keeping you nice and shaded while also making your garden look totally summery!

$29.95 at shop.nordstrom.com

6. LED String Lights

In recent years, string lights have been reclaimed from the realms of student décor and are completely acceptable in adult households now! This set of fun lights can be put anywhere in your home and are great for making summer party’s look great.

$25 at macys.com

7. Decorative Pillows

It’s surprising just how much you can breathe new life in to a room by switching out the decorative cushions. Buy a couple of these to give your living room a fun, summery feel.

$8.96 at macys.com

8. Kate Spade Dinnerware

Summer is defined by delicious, light meals and what better way to make mealtimes even more summery than by purchasing a beautiful, colorful set of Kate Spade dinnerware?

$14 to $30 at macys.com

9. Plum Blossom Flower Stem

Flowers aren’t just a spring trend! One of the easiest ways to bring the summery outdoors in to your home is by picking and choosing some appropriate flora. Something like this plum blossom flower stem is simplistic but stylish.

$19.95 at crateandbarrel.com

10. Dream Catcher

There is definitely something about summer that brings out the hippie in all of us! Show your boho tendencies with a crochet dream catcher.

$14.40 at shop.nordstrom.com

11. Funky Photo Frames

Go on your Facebook page and find some of your favorite summer photos from the past to put in these funky photo frames. They will definitely brighten up your home and evoke those amazing summer memories.

$19 to $29 at macys.com

12. Watermelon Tray

Need something to transport your cool summer drinks from the kitchen to outdoors? this watermelon painted tin tray is perfect.

$18 at shop.nordstrom.com

13. Kate Spade Glassware

You’d be surprised by just how much a new set of funky glasses can add to the summer feeling of your home. Sipping a refreshing cocktail in a great new tumbler is the perfect way to start a summer’s evening!

$50 at shop.nordstrom.com

14. Martha Stewart Cocktail Shaker

And of course, you can’t make those delicious cocktails without a fabulous cocktail shaker! This shaker from the Martha Stewart Collection is a classic, and will certainly provide you with weeks and weeks of summery fruity drinks!

$29 at macys.com

15. Inflatable Chair

Ok, don't shoot me but I'm bending the rules. I know this is $56 but it's so cool I had to share it. When you need extra seating this inflatable chair is not only super-handy but is in a nice print too. Indoors or out, no one need be left standing. Take it to beach, the pool or an outdoor concert.

$56 at shop.nordstrom.com
