# 7 Crafty YouTube Channels You Should Follow ...

If you’re a girl that’s into DIY, then you definitely need to check out these crafty YouTube channels! All of the channels included in this list not only upload videos regularly, but their tutorials are simple, easy-to-follow, fun and, most importantly, totally gorgeous! These are some crafty YouTube channels you definitely need to have in your ‘following’ list.

## 1. Debi’s Design Diary

Debi from Debi’s Design Diary says that craft has helped her through some really tough times in her life, and the passion for making things that has stemmed from that is so apparent in her videos. She is quirky and sweet and specialises in all things girly and adorable. This is definitely one of the crafty YouTube channels I could not live without!

## 2. Mark Montano

If you’re in to creating and wearing fashion, then Mark Montano’s channel is definitely for you. He creates clothes, shoes, jewellery and home decor, all with easy-to-follow instructions. Mark loves cute finds from the thrift store and has the most adorable up-cycling ideas for them! You'll definitely love this DIY channel.

## 3. Fons & Porter

Do you have a knack for sewing? Do you love cute fabrics and making adorable quilts (or want to give it a try)? Check out Fons & Porter! This channel is full of tips and tricks to get the most out of your sewing machine and not to mention super sweet quilt tutorials. Find some great material and get sewing!

## 4. Studio Knit

If you’re more comfortable with a big, soft skein of wool and a pair of knitting needles, then Studio Knit is probably more your style. As the name suggests, this channel is dedicated to all things knitted and totally cute. Not only are there projects, but there are also easy-to-follow tutorials if you’re new to the knitting game.

## 5. TheSorryGirls

TheSorryGirls are two girls called Kelsey and Becky, who are in college and love DIY! They have a super adorable hedgehog called Harley, and if that’s not enough to make you want to watch, then I don’t know what is! The girls create all sorts of adorable things, from jewellery to home decor to beauty treatments, and are totally lovely while they're doing it!

## 6. Ducttapestuff

This is by far the most unusual crafty YouTube channel on this list! Ducttapestuff is all about just that - how to create ‘stuff’ out of duct tape. Before you let that put you off, it’s actually quite cool - they make everything from school binders to purses, and even hair clips! The guy that presents the channel runs regular giveaways of groovy duct tape to use in your projects. Get taping!

## 7. JewelryTutorialHQ

The name probably makes it pretty obvious, but JewelryTutorialHQ is all about creating gorgeous pieces of jewellery, and best of all, the projects require little amounts of money! The things they create are absolutely stunning, and they’re totally simple to make. You’ll become addicted to making jewellery in no time!

Hopefully these crafty YouTube channels inspire you to get out and create beautiful things! DIY is so much fun, and it’s always great getting different ideas from all over the place. All of the tutorials that these channels make are easy to follow and don't require lots of money! Do you follow any crafty YouTube channels? What are they?
