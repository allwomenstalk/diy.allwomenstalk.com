# 8 Cool and Clever Cable Spool DIY Projects ...

From [thread spools](https://diy.allwomenstalk.com/beautifully-creative-thread-spool-craft-projects/), we go to fun and creative Cable Spool DIYs. Cable or wire spools are those cylindrical things around which cables or wires in the hardware store are... well, stored. When the store runs out of cable or wire, these spools are thrown out. In other words, you can get one (or two) for free for making one of these **cable spool DIY projects**. Craigslist is also a great resource for free cable spools.

## 1. Stool

_ Aren't these cute? These would look great in a kid's room, especially if you use bright and colorful fabric for the top part. To make these, Heather simply spray painted the spools, placed several layers of foam mattress pads on top, covered them with fabric, and added ric rac for trim.

## 3. Patio Table

![Patio Table](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2013/02/37_427x391.jpg)

This is a Ballard Designs-inspired item. Their spool table product has the whole middle section covered with rope. Desiree chose a two-tone look using sisal and Manila rope. Of course, you can also skip this part and simply add glass to the top of the table.

[Tutorial](http://camelotartcreations.blogspot.com/2011/05/ballard-designs-inspired-patio-table.html)

## 4. Mosaic Garden Table

![Mosaic Garden Table](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2013/02/46-560x457_560x457.jpg)

This **cable spool DIY** is similar to the previous idea. Instead of embellishing the spool with glass and rope, though, you will be playing with colorful tiles. To create a mosaic table, you will be needing tiles, grout, sandpaper, spatula, and a piece of cloth. This project is easy but will take a lot of time.

[Tutorial](https://bakedcottonstar.com/2011/06/20/diy-project-3-cable-spoon-mosiac-garden-table/)
