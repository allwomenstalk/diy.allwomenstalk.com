# 15 Essential Oil Diffuser Recipes to Make Your Home Smell like Spring ...

Want some **essential oil recipes to make your house smell like spring**? Essential oils are amazing! If you’ve never used them, they are definitely worth checking out. They can be used for so many things, from helping with aches and pains to perfumes to making your house smell fabulous. This article focuses on making your house smell great with recipes that remind us of spring scents.

When using a diffuser, use fresh cold water, and be sure that the diffuser itself is clean before adding water and essential oils. Start by filling it almost to the fill line with fresh water and then add in the number of drops needed for each recipe. If you want your home to smell amazing, check out these **essential oil recipes to make your house smell like spring**.

1 drop Clary Sage
1 drop Lavender
1 drop Geranium
1 drop Chamomile

## 2. Spring Breeze

3 drops Lavender
