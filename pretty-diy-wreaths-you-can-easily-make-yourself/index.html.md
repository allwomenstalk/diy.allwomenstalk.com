# 9 Pretty DIY Wreaths You Can Easily Make Yourself ...

Learning how to make a DIY wreath is a great way to save money and to keep up with the trends! DIY wreathes can be some of the prettiest out there and the best part? You can customize them how you see fit! Don’t like the color in the picture? Change it! Personally, I have made a different DIY wreath for every season and have changed it up every year! It’s a great and fun activity to plan with your spouse or your kids!

## 1. Fall Fabric Wreath

![Fall Fabric Wreath](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/11/1_fall-fabric-wreath_600x447.jpg)

This adorable DIY wreath is super chic and totally fashionable for fall! The best part about this wreath is the fact that you can change out the fabric and find one that really matches with the color scheme of your house. Don’t want to spend a lot of money on fabric? You can actually dig in the pile of discount and make this wreath affordable and totally unique!

**Tutorial:** [the36thavenue.com](https://www.the36thavenue.com/fabric-wreath-tutorial/)

## 2. Coffee Filter Wreath

![Coffee Filter Wreath](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/11/2_coffee-filter-wreath_600x840.jpg)

I actually love this DIY wreath, not only is it pretty, but it’s super easy to make. It’s made out of coffee filters ladies, how awesome is that! One note about this wreath though, keep it away from the snow and the sleet, instead, this might be a good wreath to keep inside instead of hanging it on your front door!

**Tutorial:** [lovelycraftyhome.com](https://goo.gl/mqRisL)

## 3. Grapevine Wreath

![Grapevine Wreath](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/11/3_grapevine-wreath_600x596.jpg)

This DIY wreath though is absolutely one of the best for the outdoors. This wreath is great for the wine-lovers because of the grapevines, but it can also look super fashionable and chic. This is actually the wreath that I have on my door and I love it! I also get a ton of compliments on it!

**Tutorial:** [craft-o-maniac.com](https://craft-o-maniac.com/2010/08/diy-fall-wreath.html)

## 4. Peacock Wreath

![Peacock Wreath](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/11/4_peacock-wreath_213x320.jpg)

I think that this yarn wreath is one that can add a little flare and pop to any occasion! The best part? You can switch out the colors that you have for the feathers and really make it match your color scheme. It’s also super easy to make – no, I’m serious, it is very, very easy to make this DIY wreath!

**Tutorial:** [joysoflifewithlucy.blogspot.com](http://joysoflifewithlucy.blogspot.com/2011/12/yarn-wreath-with-winter-attachment.html)

## 5. Winter Snowflake Wreath

![Winter Snowflake Wreath](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/11/5_winter-snowflake-wreath_225x281.jpg)

This DIY wreath is a little more advance, but it’s still easy. This wreath has lights entwined and while it might not be possible to place this awesome wreath outside, it can really light up a room and bring a lot of warmth in. This is my favorite winter wreath that I’ve seen and it’s done by Martha Stewart!

**Tutorial:** [marthastewart.com](https://www.marthastewart.com/908183/paper-doily-wreath?backto=true&amp;backtourl=/photogallery/holiday-wreaths)

## 6. Book Page Wreath

![Book Page Wreath](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/11/6_book-page-wreath_450x592.jpg)

Do you have a bunch of old books that you have no idea what to do with? Why not take the pages out and make an awesome wreath? This incredible wreath does take some time to make, but it is so worth it in the end, especially if it is one of your favorite books – you can hang it right on the wall in a whole new way!

**Tutorial:** [bystephanielynn.com](http://www.bystephanielynn.com/cgi-sys/suspendedpage.cgi)

## 7. Pearl Wreath

_ Do you want to brighten up a room and make it very Audrey Hepburn? This pearl wreath is the way to do it! This wreath would look ideal in a bathroom or even in a girl’s bedroom, with a Paris theme. In fact, in my next house, I think this is going to be a focal point! It’s easy and ever-so-stylish!

**Tutorial:** bhg.com_

## 8. Cranberry Wreath

![Cranberry Wreath](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/11/8_cranberry-wreath_599x640.jpg)

With Thanksgiving having just past, it’s a great time to get all of those extra cranberries and put them to good use! A cranberry wreath is totally one of the staples at my parent’s house for Thanksgiving and this year, I made it myself! This tutorial is very easy to follow and makes it super easy to make this DIY wreath!

**Tutorial:** [hisugarplum.blogspot.com](http://hisugarplum.blogspot.com/2010/12/christmas-cranberry-wreath.html)

## 9. Snowball Wreath

![Snowball Wreath](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/11/9_snowball-wreath_400x267.jpg)

Finally girls, if you want to get really crafty, why not make yourself a unique and different snowball wreath? It’s super festive, totally holiday and it’s so chic! I love all of the different colors and I love the fact that it is a bit chunky. Try it out and let us know how you like it!
