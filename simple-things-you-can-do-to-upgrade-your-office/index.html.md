# 7 Simple Things You Can do to Upgrade Your Office ...

Want to discover a few very simple things you can do to upgrade your office to increase your motivation and productivity? You would be surprised how some simple things can help you stay focused on your work for extended periods of time. Your office should be the place where you feel energized, productive and extremely creative, so try to focus on making your office more comfortable, more professional and easier to use, with the right tools at your fingertips. Here are some simple things you can do to upgrade your office for maximum results with minimum effort:

## 1. Add Extra Storage Space

![Add Extra Storage Space](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k5/i8/lj0e36f8_600x540.jpg)

If your goal is to upgrade your office to create the perfect workspace, the easiest thing you can do is to simply add some extra storage space. This way, you will get rid of all the clutter and get easier access to all those things you need to do your job more efficiently. You could add a small drawer to your desk, you could build a few shelves or you could even use some boxes or baskets to store the things you don’t use very often.

## 2. Add More Light

![Add More Light](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xc/w5/h0togycn_481x640.jpg)

It’s extremely important for your office to be well lit for the sake of your eyesight health. A lot of studies have shown that low light conditions can make you feel drowsy and even slightly depressed, so add more light to help you focus much better on your tasks. If possible, position your desk next to your window to get as much natural light as possible, if not, buy a good lamp that will provide you with enough light.

## 3. Add Some Color

![Add Some Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zt/oo/rq53fy60_600x782.jpg)

What better way to refresh your office than by adding some color? This will definitely make you feel more energized, more comfortable and why not, even happier. Just add some bright colors to spice things up a bit. You could paint your walls,add a few pieces of brightly colored furniture like some chairs, a joyful painting, an interesting rug or even some colorful office supplies. You will transform your office into a fun work area that will help you stay focused and where you’ll be able to spend a lot of time without feeling sleepy, tired, bored, depressed or even claustrophobic. .

## 4. Find a Comfortable Chair

![Find a Comfortable Chair](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o8/ua/j0754hip_600x901.jpg)

It’s extremely important to sit in the right position while you are working. Find a comfortable, ergonomic chair that provides lumbar support and which you can adjust based on your own needs. Experts say that you should make sure your knees and elbows are bent at 90 degree angles and that the armrest provides proper support for your forearms. I believe this is one of the items in your office you should select very carefully, since it has such a huge effect on your well-being, both mental and physical.

## 5. Put Your Monitor at Eye Level

![Put Your Monitor at Eye Level](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cv/uw/bf1w0a5u_600x900.jpg)

If you don’t want to look like the hunchback of Notre-Dame in a couple of years, then you’d better put your monitor at eye level. Well, all jokes aside, it’s extremely important to keep your neck and back straight when you’re looking at your screen. If you usually prefer to work at a laptop, it’s best to buy a USB keyboard and put the laptop somewhere higher, at your eye level. I’m sure you’ll find a solution to this, since there are a lot of things that will allow you to do it. You could even use a stack of books if you don’t have any other option at that moment.

## 6. Get a Plant

![Get a Plant](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kn/au/uw2n0pg9_550x550.jpg)

In order to update your office and cheer yourself up, you could just get a small potted plant that will bring you joy every time you look at it, that will provide you with some extra oxygen and that will help you stay connected with nature, so you won’t feel like you’re in a prison all day long. If you don’t want to spend too much time looking after it, get a small plant that doesn’t require too much sunlight and which is easy to maintain. You could try something like Peace Lilies, English Ivy, Philodendrons or succulents.

## 7. Surround Yourself with Things That Make You Happy

![Surround Yourself with Things That Make You Happy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jv/ym/gxsgnuy1_600x699.jpg)

In order to boost your productivity and upgrade your office, the best thing you can do is to simply surround yourself with things that make you happy, that bring you joy and that remind you how lucky you are just to be yourself. You could bring a picture of your loved ones and look at it every time you’re feeling a little bit down. Also, hang your favorite painting on the wall or you could even get a small fish tank. Just surround yourself with things that put your mind at ease, that make you feel relaxed and that will provide you with the energy you need to take care of all your tasks.

There are a lot of things you can do to upgrade your office and turn it into a more pleasant work environment. I just mentioned a few here but I’m sure there are many more I forgot about. Can you give me a hand and tell me if you know any other simple things you can do to upgrade your office? Please share your thoughts in the comments section!
