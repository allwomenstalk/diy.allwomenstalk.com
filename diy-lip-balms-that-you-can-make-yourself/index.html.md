# 8 DIY Lip Balms That You Can Make Yourself ...

In your quest to rid your life of harmful chemicals, you've likely thought about creating your own beauty products. We are here to push you in the right direction by giving you DIY lip balm recipes. The following DIYs showcase mixes and flavors that are good for your lips. They look quite easy to make, too!

## 1. Start Simple

_ For those who want no-frills DIY lip balm, this is the recipe for you. Basically, this beauty recipe calls only for two ingredients: beeswax and coconut oil. Other materials that you will need are empty tubes where you can put your finished product, toothpicks, a funnel, and a double broiler.

Source: woodsofbelltrees.com_

## 2. Add Your Favorite Essential Oils

![food,dessert,petal,produce,heart,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kp/g2/cx9le_food_dessert_petal_produce_heart_600x532.jpg)

This recipe calls for a couple more ingredients compared to the first we featured but it's still an easy project. Apart from the beeswax and coconut oil, shea butter and essential oil are also added. A note on supplies: make sure that you use items or tools that are specifically for your cosmetic-making adventures.

Source: [wecanmakeanything.net](http://www.wecanmakeanything.net/2015/02/diy-lip-balm.html)

## 3. Sprinkle in Some Honey

![food,produce,flavor,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z8/kl/ykgv3_food_produce_flavor_600x400.jpg)

Here is another DIY lip balm recipe that requires only a short list of ingredients. You will need beeswax, coconut oil, and honey for this fun and simple recipe. You will also need a few drops of vitamin E oil for better skin protection.

Source: [dreamalittlebigger.com](https://www.dreamalittlebigger.com/post/beeswax-honey-lip-balm.html)

## 4. Add Natural Coloring

![,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mt/dx/y8fxwpvl5542d2446c80b984792114.jpg__600x391.jpg)

If you want your lip balm to give your pretty lips a nice tint, you will want to add another ingredient to your beauty recipe. We don't suggest using artificial chemicals. You can go natural by using beetroot powder. You will want to get the powder at an aromatherapy supplier with known organic merchandise. You can make your own beetroot powder but take note that there might be a trial and error phase.

Source: [blahblahmagazine.com.au](http://blahblahmagazine.com.au/homemade-tinted-lip-balm/)

## 5. Make It Floral

_ Here is a recipe perfect for a lovely tropical theme. This recipe calls for dried hibiscus. You will find that there is a good number of dried flowers that you can use for making your DIY lip balm. Aside from the dried flowers, this recipe also calls for beeswax, coconut oil, and orange essential oil.

Source: woodsofbelltrees.com_

## 6. Make Your Own Chapstick

![medicine,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ws/vf/nw92f_medicine_600x401.jpg)

This is another simple recipe. This doesn't come with tints or flavors. It's fantastic for protecting your lips during dry days, too. To make your own batch, you will be using cocoa butter and olive oil mixed with beeswax and coconut oil.

Source: [littlehomebyhand.wordpress.com](https://littlehomebyhand.wordpress.com/2011/10/13/making-chapstick/)

## 7. Yes, You Can Add Chocolate, Too

![leaf,branch,jewellery,flower,produce,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z9/p4/q0r5w_leaf_branch_jewellery_flower_produce_600x400.jpg)

For those who want a lip balm that could practically double as dessert, this is the perfect recipe for you. To make your own, you will add chocolate chips to the wax and oil mixture. Several drops of vanilla extract will go a long way, too.

Source: [hellonatural.co](https://helloglow.co/honey-lip-balm-lip-scrub/)

## 8. Make It Minty

![food,dish,plant,produce,citrus,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g5/xv/es27r_food_dish_plant_produce_citrus_520x346.jpg)

Mint is another popular flavor for lip balms. Since there are fun recipes out there for DIY mint-flavored lip balms, we suggest avoiding tubes at the store. They come with chemicals that could harm your skin. Besides, cooking your own will take you less than an hour.

Source: [afreshlegacy.net](https://goo.gl/C8cFsa)

Go ahead and discard your store-bought tubes or tubs. Get yourself some beeswax and have fun coming up with your very own blend of lip balm. Who knows, you might just become the next organic beauty mogul.
