What could be more fun than DIY Pallet crafts? If you keep your eyes open, you can often find pallets for free laying by the side of the road or being given away. 1 Planter

Via DIY Vertical Gardening | 8 ...
Rig a pallet to stand up and you can use to plant flowers, succulents and herbs in it. This is one of the coolest DIY pallet crafts around!

***

For the green-thumbed DIY enthusiast, this creative project is not just space-efficient, but it also breathes life into any room. Saving you the trouble of bulky pots, this pallet planter gives a chic rustic appeal to the vertical garden concept. Additionally, you can customize the color to match your interior decor, or add wheels for easy mobility, making your garden as functional as it is attractive. A true conversation starter, this piece showcases your plants in an eye-catching display, marrying form and function with an eco-friendly twist.
