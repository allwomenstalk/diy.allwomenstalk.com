***

Repeating patterns add an elegant symmetry to your bracelet designs, offering a visually pleasing aesthetic. Whether you opt for a sequence of monochromatic buttons or a mix of vibrant, colorful ones, the consistency of a pattern can transform simple buttons into a fashionable accessory.
