# 20 Festive Thanksgiving Crafts  for Your Family ...

What better way to celebrate Turkey Day than with some fun Thanksgiving crafts? Kids especially will love putting together some fun projects that you can use to decorate your house. These Thanksgiving crafts are also a great way to keep a houseful of kids busy while the turkey cooks. Of course, they're also going to be loads of fun for you, so get ready for some memories and some laughs with your family.

## 1. Homemade Vase

![Homemade Vase](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ge/id/jo758zi6_375x500.jpg)

Source: [15 Easy DIY Fall Centerpieces](https://www.countryliving.com/diy-crafts/g2009/fall-centerpieces/)
This is the time of year to find all your craft supplies in the backyard. Thanksgiving crafts are limitless with a pile of sticks and leaves from your lawn.

## 2. Thankful Pumpkin

![Thankful Pumpkin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/su/xt/uojkiqvp_415x503.jpg)

Source: [The Moffatt Girls: Easy I ...](http://moffattgirls.blogspot.com/2013/10/easy-i-am-thankful-pumpkin-craft.html)

## 3. Fingerprint Leaves

![Fingerprint Leaves](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w5/bu/n33lm5sl_426x640.jpg)

Source: [Little Page Turners: Colorful Leaf ...](http://littlepageturners.blogspot.com/2011/11/colorful-leaf-printing.html)

## 4. Adorable Wreath

![Adorable Wreath](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r3/u1/drc3xszq_600x581.jpg)

## 5. Candy Corn Turkey

## 6. Lego Stamped Indian Corn

## 7. Leaf Turkey

Source: [Leaf Turkey Craft for Kids ...](https://www.craftymorning.com/leaf-turkey-craft-kids/)

## 15. Fruit Turkey

## 16. Glitter Leaves

_ Source: Make Your Own Autumn Leaves ..._
I love glitter, don't you? Grab some leaves from the backyard and make them shine, then hang them all over your house.

## 17. Toilet Paper Tube Turkey

![Toilet Paper Tube Turkey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xz/nh/gywjuo6k_570x762.jpg)

## 18. Pinecone Decoration
