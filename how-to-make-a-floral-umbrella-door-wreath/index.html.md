Do you always like to decorate your front door? Do you like to do crafts? Would you like to maybe even make someone a nice gift? Would you like to make some spare money by selling something pretty on the side? Want to learn **how to make a floral umbrella door wreath**? Look no further than this video!

## 1. Supplies Needed

All you need is an umbrella with a curved handle, ribbon, scissors and bunches of fake flowers.

## 2. Types of Umbrellas

The only requirement is that the umbrella has to have a curved handle.

## 3. Characteristics

Do you realize how many umbrella flower door wreathes you could make that are all different? Mix and match colors. Mix and match patterns. Mix and match flowers. Mix and match the ribbon.

## 4. Cost

Umbrellas can be cheap or expensive. Shop according to your own budget. The rest of the supplies may be bought at Dollar Tree! Woo Hoo!

## 5. Purpose

What is known about spring weather and upcoming summer weather? Rain showers and rain storms! Why not celebrate the rain?

This is a craft for you to be proud of! A craft that can look professional yet made with hardly any effort and minimal cash.
