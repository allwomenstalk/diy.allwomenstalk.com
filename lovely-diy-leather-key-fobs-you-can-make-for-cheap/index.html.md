# 8 Chic DIY Leather Key Fobs You Can Make on a Budget

DIY leather key fobs are probably some of the simplest and most charming things you can create using the material. For anyone who has been thinking about dabbling in a bit of leatherwork, starting with DIY leather key fobs is a wonderful idea. This way, your introduction to leather will result in lovely and functional products you can use every day. Happy crafting!

## 1. Stamped Names

![Stamped Names](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wi/y4/nh/z4dmv0zw_600x400.jpg)

How beautiful are these DIY leather key fobs? They would make for thoughtful gifts for friends during the holidays or for any special event. They are quite easy to make, too. For the names, you will be needing a leather stamp set. Leather stamps come in different designs and are typically available in any leather store or online craft shop.

**Tutorial Source:** [thesweetestoccasion.com](https://www.thesweetestoccasion.com/2013/11/diy-stamped-leather-keychains/)

## 2. Mustache

![Mustache](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/03/rw/sm/f4drigwa_400x298.jpg)

Mustaches may no longer be the height of fashion trends, but they still make for fun shapes when crafting leather key fobs. For this particular project, you will also need a thick needle, pliers, thread, a sewing machine, and a pair of scissors. If you don't have a sewing machine, you can hand-sew your key fobs easily, adding a unique personal touch.

**Tutorial Source:** [etsyrussianteam.blogspot.com](https://etsyrussianteam.blogspot.com/2011/06/moustache-keyfob.html)

| Project Type | Skill Level | Estimated Time |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Stamped Key Fobs | Beginner | 15-20 mins |
| Tassel Key Fobs | Beginner | 10-15 mins |
| Animal Face Fobs | Intermediate | 30-40 mins |

## 3. Tassel

![Tassel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ff/m5/e8/x3gmeq1l_550x374.jpg)

Gorgeous! This is probably my favorite among these handmade leather key ring crafts. Making these is easy, too. You slit a piece of leather repeatedly, roll and glue along the top, and then embellish with glittery ribbon and thread. It's a fun way to create [handmade gift ideas](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-chic-diy-gift-ideas-that-look-expensive/) for your loved ones.

**Tutorial Source:** [missrenaissance.com](https://missrenaissance.com/2013/04/01/6697/)

## 4. Fun Shapes

![Fun Shapes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pt/r7/zo/bj6m6joe_599x395.jpg)

This handmade leather key fob tutorial is much more basic than the previous ones, but it is still a lot of fun. It teaches you how to attach rivets as well, so this particular DIY is very useful for future [leather craft projects](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-simple-diy-leather-projects-to-try-this-weekend/). Aside from the fun shapes you see above, you can experiment with making your key fobs in other forms, such as initials or stars.

**Tutorial Source:** [transientexpression.com](http://transientexpression.com/diy-leather-keyfob-tutorial/)

## 5. Neon

![Neon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zs/zq/r0/vr1x8kjg_600x448.jpg)

For this DIY leather key fob, you will be employing the techniques you have learned from the previous tutorials. After constructing the strip of leather into a key fob, you then paint it using neon colors or any hue you prefer. Geometric and Aztec patterns will look fantastic and add a modern pop to your accessories.

**Tutorial Source:** [sasandrose.com](http://www.sasandrose.com/2013/11/diy-leather-key-fob.html)

- Leather scraps or faux leather

- Sharp scissors or a rotary cutter

- Leather stamps and a mallet

- Key rings and jump rings

- Leather adhesive or a sewing machine

## 6. Stamped Hearts

![Stamped Hearts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w8/f8/un/b1pkr917_600x399.jpg)

The idea behind this handmade leather key fob craft project is similar to the first, but instead of a strip, you will be working with heart shapes. Aside from letters, you can also stamp your lovely key ring with other fun symbols, such as arrows. These are perfect [easy DIY accessories](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-easy-diy-fashion-accessories-to-refresh-your-wardrobe/) to brighten up your day.

**Tutorial Source:** [polkadotchair.com](https://www.polkadotchair.com/leather-conversation-hearts-valentines-day-diy-gift-idea/)

## 7. Animal Faces

![Animal Faces](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nw/p8/kr/jdi0fvvi_600x398.jpg)

This is the cutest! Constructing this looks complicated, but it is not that difficult. To make the face look more 3D, you simply have to cut a slit from the top of the head to the nose. After embroidering the snout details and punching holes for the eyes, you then overlap the slit, add an eyelet and a ring, and that is it. I bet your family will want to snatch these up from your craft table right away.

**Tutorial Source:** [pm-betweenthelines.blogspot.com](http://pm-betweenthelines.blogspot.com/2012/09/animal-key-chain-diy.html)

## 8. Stuffed

![Stuffed](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h6/hi/vo/wl155ml5_600x400.jpg)

These are made of faux leather, sure, but they still deserve a spot on this list. If you are keen on having fun colors for your DIY key fob or you are vegan, we suggest going the faux leather route. They are much easier to work with, in my experience, and are often vastly cheaper. They are a great choice for many [modern DIY projects](#4).

**Tutorial Source:** [lovelyindeed.com](https://lovelyindeed.com/diy-leather-shape-keychains/)
