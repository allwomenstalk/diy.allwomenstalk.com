# 8 Delightfully Unique Memo Boards That You Can Make for Cheap ...

Looking for a way to spice up your home office or dorm room? A unique and darling pin or memo board is just the ticket. There are probably hundreds of ways to DIY memo boards. However, if you're in the mood for something one-of-a-kind, something that will put a smile on your face day in and day out, we suggest looking into the following delightfully unique DIY pin boards.

## 1. Belt It up

![Belt It up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k0/dc/hqqfil3p556b95af18f80390777800_600x900.jpg)

This is one rustic and lovely DIY memo board. You can make this without spending a lot of money. You can use thin belts that you are no longer using for this project. A quick trip to the flea market will give you cheap belt shopping opportunities, too. Other materials that you will need are wooden boards, wood glue, upholstery nail heads, and picture hanger hardware

Source: [abeautifulmess.com](https://abeautifulmess.com/make-this-belt-strap-memo-board-1/)

## 2. Make It Fruity

![Make It Fruity](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zn/cf/h1kt0uvp556b95b053da3070257057_600x900.jpg)

One of the fun ways to make your own unique DIY pin board is to give a boring cork board a makeover. And one of the more enjoyable ways to go about this is to take the fruity route. A watermelon design is a perfect example. For this project, you will need only craft paints, paint brush, and some markers.

Source: [orangenmond.at](https://www.orangenmond.at/blog/2015/04/29/diy-wassermelonen-memo-board/)

## 3. Combine Chalkboard and Burlap

![Combine Chalkboard and Burlap](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ch/pl/w0psbnsb556b95b0e6daf712920819_600x752.jpg)

Chalkboard paint and burlap are popular crafting materials. You can use both to make your very own unique memo board. I am loving the chalkboard wooden cup here. You can get one at your nearest craft supply store. There are probably other shape that you can choose from if you're not too keen on the cup idea.

Source: [decorandthedog.net](https://web.archive.org/web/http://decorandthedog.net/decorandthedog/2014/2/9/diy-burlap-memo-board-michaelshometalk-pinterest-party)

## 4. Be Inspired by Downton Abbey

![Be Inspired by Downton Abbey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ha/s9/jm4w63p9556b95b086516905816042_492x748.jpg)

This is a memo board inspired by the butler bell pull of Downton Abbey. Perfect, right? I can just imagine Carson nodding in that quiet, dignified way to show his approval of this fun DIY project. To make your own, you will only need a wooden plaque, a metal bookplate, doorbell button, a brass ring drawer pull, and a bulldog clip. You will also need black paint to change the color of the wood plaque.

Source: [attagirlsays.com](https://www.attagirlsays.com/downton-abbey-inspired-butlers-bell-memo-board/)

## 5. Upcycle a Window

![Upcycle a Window](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r3/kl/x9a45ny1556b95b1eee1a556954438_599x600.jpg)

Another cheap way to make your unique DIY memo board is to upcycle a window. You can use one stored in your garage or grab a small one at the hardware store. The steps here involve removing one glass section to be replaced with a fabric covered cork board. Drawer knobs were added to the bottom part as well for additional features.

Source: [mysocalledcraftylife.com](https://www.mysocalledcraftylife.com/2013/10/10/diy-vintage-upcycled-window-memo-board/)

## 6. Cut a Monogram from Cork

![Cut a Monogram from Cork](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xv/sa/rd3dly3k556b95b319105532048918_600x900.jpg)

This DIY project is so simple and easy, you might just end up making the whole alphabet! Basically, you will be cutting a cork sheet or thin board following a letter template. You can also do a word, instead of a monogram. Examples: WORK or PLAY.

Source: [sarahhearts.com](https://www.sarahhearts.com/monogram-cork-board-wall-tiles/)

## 7. Make is Geometric

![Make is Geometric](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mo/nn/sk8kh2uk556b95b383a9a651807407_600x621.jpg)

How beautiful! This is my favorite from these unique DIY memo boards. If you want to make your version, you will be needing a polyester pin board, some paint, and some ribbon or command strips for hanging. Tools needed include scissors, ruler, pencil, and tape. As far as colors are concerned, I think going the loud and cheerful way would be great. Imagine this in bright pinks and teals. Fun, right?

Source: [crafthunter.com.au](http://crafthunter.com.au/diy-diamond-pin-board/)

## 8. Use a Honeycomb Pattern

_ Remember when the honeycomb pattern was all the rage? If you still happen to be in love with it, you can use the design for your home office or dorm pin board. Materials needed for this project include foam board and contact paper. You will also need compass, ruler, and pencil to make a precise hexagon template.

Source: apartmenttherapy.com_?

Give your home office or dorm a much-needed update with the DIY memo boards above. Something unique is the perfect way to show off your fascinating personality, for sure.
