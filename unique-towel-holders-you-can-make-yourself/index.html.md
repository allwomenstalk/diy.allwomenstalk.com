# 7 Unique Towel Holders You Can Make Yourself ...

If you are having storage issues in your bathroom (whether you have a small or a large bathroom) you might think of unique DIY towel holders to keep away some of the clutter. You don’t need to spend a fortune if you want to redecorate your bathroom. You can use things found in nature or you can recycle old materials. Just be creative, have fun and add some character to your bathroom. Here are 7 unique DIY towel holders that will inspire you to look at a towel holder in a whole new way:

## 1. Turn a Headboard into a Towel Rack

![Turn a Headboard into a Towel Rack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tj/fc/h8dqespw_600x832.jpg)

If you are looking for unique DIY towel holders, then this is one of the easiest projects you can choose. Just use an old headboard, cut the legs off with a saw, sand down the headboard, prime and paint it and then just add hardware with a drill. If you can’t find a headboard, you could simply use a plain piece of wood.

## 2. Rope-Shaped Towel Holders

![Rope-Shaped Towel Holders](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ql/ne/tu263b7w_520x650.jpg)

It’s very easy to do this rope-shaped towel holder, since everyone can tie knots, right? Just use a ½ - inch natural-fiber rope from a home-supply store and secure a series of large screw eyes to the wall, then simply feed the rope through and knot the ends. It really is that simple and it looks gorgeous too.

## 3. Old Wooden Hangers as Towel Bars

![Old Wooden Hangers as Towel Bars](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p5/rk/oo6nxsk4_500x666.jpg)

Vintage objects can have a lot of clever and practical uses. For example, you can repurpose two old wooden hangers as towel bars. Create a sturdy place to hang a towel by anchoring a piece of dowel to the wall before attaching a hanger with screws.

## 4. A Tree Holding Towels on Its Limbs

![A Tree Holding Towels on Its Limbs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y5/lh/jfjdvtit_450x716.jpg)

This is such a clever idea, especially for a kids bathroom! It’s not that hard to do either; just use a wall sticker or if you are talented, you can draw the tree yourself, then add some hooks and secure them with some screws. This is an adorable idea that will brighten your bathroom!

## 5. Use a Ladder

![Use a Ladder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zl/fu/l78ofgis_400x420.jpg)

If you don’t like to or don’t know how to use tools but you still want to make a beautiful holder for your towels, you could simply use a ladder made from bamboo (or some other type of wood that can withstand humidity). Paint it and them simply put it somewhere in your bathroom where you think it would look best.

## 6. Octagonal Towel Holder

![Octagonal Towel Holder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gg/f7/m3v8wwzt_444x676.jpg)

For this project you use a wire clothes hanger and a scrap of leather. Start by straightening out your hanger and bend at 4 inches all the way around. Then simply glue that onto the leather hanger. The result is very beautiful and very practical at the same time.

## 7. Rustic Towel Rack

_ You can make a multi-level towel rack with 2x4s’ cut into five 12-inch sections. You simply have to nail the wood to stained mounting strips fastened to the wall. This is an amazing way to store rolled towels and it provides a beautiful rustic look too!

There are a lot of ways to create beautiful towel holders. I just mentioned a few but I’m sure that there are many more I could add to this list. Do you know any other unique DIY towel holders ideas? Please tell us about them in the comments section!
