# These 21 Crochet Projects Are Perfect for Beginners ...

I'm just learning to crochet, and I can't get enough! I've spent precious Netflix time searching for crochet patterns for beginners instead of actually watching the show, and there's no end in sight. Here are a few of the patterns I've found and I can't wait to try. Eep! (To see the pattern and instructions, give the image a tap.)

## 1. Simple Bow

![Simple Bow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l1/lg/xzexenll56b3799eee9c6277346001_600x400.jpg)

[crochetingthedayaway.blogspot.com](http://crochetingthedayaway.blogspot.com/2012/01/simple-bow-pattern.html)
This would be so cute for a little baby boy (bowtie) or on a little girl's headband!

![Pineapple Garland](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xi/zb/cpxzd4ws56b37a03d5a39188120193_600x450.jpg)

## 6. Flower Coasters
