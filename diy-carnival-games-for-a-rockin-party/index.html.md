# 31 DIY Carnival Games for a Rockin' Party ...

You can recreate all the fun of the fair with DIY carnival games. It's always fun to go to a party with games, whatever your age. If you're having a themed party you can set the games up to co-ordinate with that theme, otherwise just go bright and colorful. Ready to flex your crafting muscles with some great ideas for DIY carnival games?

If you are throwing a [Star Wars theme party](https://www.party-expert.com/collections/star-wars/), just style any of the following in Star Wars esthetic and it will be a rage!

Via [Free Carnival Party Printables](https://goo.gl/Ppeh9i)

## 6. Balloon Darts

![pink,balloon,toy,DARTS,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/au/cr/vgvcc_pink_balloon_toy_darts_600x800.jpg)

![](https://img.allw.mn/thumbs/21/67mjw4iz9o4h8hkvmjcu3_1857x1168.jpg)

In addition to being used for balloon darts games,[custom balloons](http://customballoons.com/)can also be used to decorate your carnival party scene. This is very dreamy and interesting!
