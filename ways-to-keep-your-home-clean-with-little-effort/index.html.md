# 6 Super Savvy Ways to Keep Your Home Clean with Little Effort ...

Are you looking for **ways to keep your home clean with little effort**? I think everyone can agree that we all want a clean home but NONE of us like the actual cleaning part of it. That’s where you’re in luck! I’ve got 6 super savvy **ways to keep your home clean with little effort**.

## 1. Keep It Simple

You can make cleaning easy by not over thinking it. Many of us have learned from parents or others that cleaning must be long and tedious to be done right, but this is simply not true. Finding shortcuts and easy to use tools can remove some of the hassle that comes with keeping your home clean. Keeping it simple is one of the very best **ways to keep your home clean with little effort**.

## 2. Better, Not Perfect

Often, we feel we must be extremely thorough when we clean, but this mindset usually means that we end up cleaning for hours to make everything perfect. If the goal is better, not perfect, cleaning becomes less of a chore. This is especially true if you learn some tricks that make cleaning quicker. Rather than cleaning thoroughly each time, step 3 will help you clean smarter not harder.

## 3. Frequency Beats Quality

Cleaning daily not only reduces the effort needed for cleaning, but it also improves the likelihood that areas that were previously missed will get cleaned on another day.

## 4. Clean Smarter Not Harder

The tools that we use to clean can either make the cleaning process short or long. Using disposable cleaning cloths and similar tools that combine steps can make cleaning much less time-consuming.

## 5. Share the Love

Get the family involved in keeping your home clean. Assigning tasks to specific individuals each day not only takes the burden off one person but also ensures that cleaning is easier because it is done frequently.

## 6. Get Rid of the Clutter

Getting family members into the habit of putting things back immediately after using them keeps the clutter down and makes cleaning less overwhelming. When several items must be put away before cleaning can be done, people often become overwhelmed and avoid cleaning. Getting in the habit of going through your home before going to bed to make sure everything has been picked up can make keeping your home organized easier.
