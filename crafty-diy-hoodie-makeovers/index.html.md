# 10 Crafty DIY Hoodie Makeovers ...

From patterns for hooded pieces of clothing and accessories, we move to crafty DIY Hoodie Makeovers. These craft projects will call for a wide variety of materials. Some of these are geared towards kiddie style while others are more grown-up chic. From cute to edgy, these **DIY hoodie makeovers** will surely make any fan of altered fashion very happy.

## 1. Fabric Applique

![Fabric Applique](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/10/14_500x383.jpg)

This **DIY hoodie makeover** is inspired by Anthropologie sweaters. Since their products are ridiculously overpriced, turning to your fabric stash for refashioning help is the best decision. Here Alisa shows us how to alter a hoodie with the help of cute fabric blocks.

[Tutorial](https://alisaburke.blogspot.com/2010/07/altered-hoodie-tutorial.html)

## 2. Octopus

![Octopus](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/10/25-560x374_560x374.jpg)

This is all sorts of awesome. If you have a kid who is obsessed about all things underwater, making him or her this octopus hoodie is highly recommended. The great thing about this particular DIY is that you will be using two pieces of unused clothing. So basically, not only will you be making a cute hoodie, you'll be recycling, too.

[Tutorial](http://nestfullofeggs.blogspot.com/2012/01/octopus-hoodie-tutorial.html)

## 3. Studded Sleeves

[img.allw.mn](https://img.allw.mn/content/diy/2012/10/3_400x266.png)

Here is one of the** hoodie makeovers** that is on the edgy side. To make this, you will be needing a plain-colored hoodie, flat head studs, jewelry pliers, and fabric glue. The process is simple: you pierce the studs along the sleeves of the hoodie and glue the prongs underneath to the fabric.

[Tutorial](https://goo.gl/mPv83g)

## 4. Ruffled

![Ruffled](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/10/45-560x280_560x280.jpg)

Got a plain and boring hoodie that you wish looks a lot more feminine? Pre-made ruffle trims is your best friend. You simple sew this to the edge of the hoodie head covering and you're done.

[Tutorial](http://seekatesew.blogspot.com/2011/01/diy-ruffle-hoodie-self-portrait-style.html)

## 5. Ruffled II

![Ruffled II](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/10/55_493x369.jpg)

This is another ruffled **DIY hoodie makeover** but the embellishments are not limited to the hood; the ruffles extend to the front opening of the hoodie. I love the printed fabric used here. Pretty, right?

[Tutorial](https://www.ashleyannphotography.com/blog/2010/10/28/diy-ruffled-jacket/)

## 6. Applique and Ruffles

_ This DIY sewing refashion project involves some of the methods I already mentioned here. Specifically, you will be adding applique to the back and ruffles along the hood and zipper opening.

Tutorial_

## 7. Vertebrae Studs

![Vertebrae Studs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/10/75_450x280.jpg)

Here we have another **hoodie refashion** that will involve the use of studs. Instead of embellishing the sleeves, though, you will be adding metal details along the spine and hood of the sweater. With the addition of silver chain, this is one altered hoodie that is all sorts of HOT.

[Tutorial](http://www.maisondesreveries.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 8. Dino

![Dino](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/10/85_400x346.jpg)

I love this! I bet even adult males will love this type of hoodie refashion. To make one like it, you will need a hooded sweater, felt, scissors, needle, thread, and an iron. The felt is the material you will use for making the dinosaur vertebrae details.

[Tutorial](http://www.whateverdeedeewants.com/2012/04/diy-dinosaur-hoodie-sweatshirt.html)

## 9. Woven

![Woven](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/10/93_450x280.jpg)

For this hoodie refashion, you can either add the woven details to the back of a pre-made sweater or construct a new hoodie from scratch. If you choose the latter, [THIS](https://diy.allwomenstalk.com/fantastic-diy-hoodie-craft-projects/) pattern may help.

[Tutorial](http://pretty-ditty.blogspot.com/2012/04/diy-hoodie-weave.html)

## 10. Resized

![Resized](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/10/103_450x280.jpg)

This particular **hoodie makeover** is another brilliant idea from Kate. It is a fantastic way to make use of your boyfriend's discarded sweatshirt.

[Tutorial](http://seekatesew.blogspot.com/2011/10/sweatshirt-liposuction.html)

I hope that these **DIY hoodie makeovers** make you more ready for the cold days coming our way! If you have more stylish ideas, don't hesitate to share them with us.
