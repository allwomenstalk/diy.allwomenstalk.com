# This DIY Gift Idea is Pure Genius ...

These are so cute and nice to give as presents. Similar to making kitchen magnets, they are easy and inexpensive. Would you like to give something unique for a Birthday, anniversary or any occasion? Here you go...

These photo coasters only take a few hours of your time, mostly waiting for the Mod Podge to dry.

When finished, you have a quality and professional product. You could also make this a hobby and possibly make some money from. Why not?

Although the supplies may be obviously bought from a craft store, why not go shopping at a hardware or a home improvement store?

Have a wonderful time creating and maybe release some stress while doing so.
