# Video Guide for How to Make a DIY Pamper Me Jar ...

Want to know **how to make a DIY pamper jar**? This is the perfect gift for the ladies in your life and may be made with not only items shown but even items not shown. This YouTube video shows you the basics then you take it from there! I will give you some of my suggestions. The sky is the limit!

Is it your BFF's or your mother's birthday? Do you need favors for a shower or party? Do you need a hostess gift? Take a peek at the video and learn **how to make a DIY pamper jar**.

QueWendy

Published on January 25, 2015

All of the contents may be found at Dollar Tree, such as the mason jars, lip glosses, cotton balls, nail polish remover, nail polish, nail clipper and file set.

Personally, I would include travel-size lotion, chocolates, shower pouf, travel-size fragrance and maybe a ribbon wrapped around the jar.

This is her first DIY tutorial and I think she did a very good job. She explained a very cute gift and a useful concept.

When making the pamper me jar, do as you would like to receive. Do you like the colors of the lip gloss? Are the lip glosses and the nail polish matching? Is the fragrance actually pretty? Would you be excited?

Consider this video as an affordable and unique suggestion in women's gift-giving!
