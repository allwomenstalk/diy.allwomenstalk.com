# 7 Tips to Spring Clean Your House Quickly ...

I don’t know about you but I am all for tips to spring clean your house quickly. I want to get that necessary chore over and done with so I can get back to my life. And I think the sooner, the better! So I have found these little tips to spring clean your house quickly that I would love to share with you. Hopefully it will make your spring cleaning go a little faster!

## 1. Do Your Walls with a Mop

One of the very best tips to spring clean your house is to wash your walls with a mop instead of by hand. This saves you so very much time! Now that I have learned this time saving trick, it is so much easier to wash my walls down. I can do the work in a quarter of the time it used to take me. That always makes me happy.

## 2. Hire Your Carpets Done

I believe if you have carpet, it is just easier and better to hire them done. For one thing, the professionals know what they are doing. I think they are much more experienced at stain removal than I am. This is one service I am much more willing to pay for rather than doing it myself. It is also very time-saving.

## 3. Enlist Help

Spring cleaning is too much for one person to do alone if you share a home. Enlist help. Have everyone pitch in and help and you can get it done so much faster. As the old saying goes, “Many hands make light work.” It is proven true, time and again.

## 4. Do Mini Blinds in the Bathtub

Oh, mini blinds are such a pain. I think this is certainly the very worst part about spring cleaning. But I have learned a little trick that makes it so much easier. Take them down and wash them in the bathtub. They come so much cleaner, so much faster.

## 5. Do All the Curtains at the Same Time

I think it is best to do all of your curtains at the same time. Just take the day to take them down, wash them and hang them back up. But it is best to always check the instructions on how to launder them first. They may be dry clean only curtains. You should always follow the instructions on the label. Trust me; I have learned that lesson from experience.

## 6. Buy a Ceiling Fan Cleaner

There is a better way to clean your ceiling fan than climbing up and taking it all apart. There is actually a device that you can buy that cleans your ceiling fan. It is like a duster but formed to fit over each blade. You do still have to clean the globes but it helps cut down on the work tremendously. It also saves you a great deal of time.

## 7. Use the Self-cleaning Oven Option

Why, oh why, would someone clean their oven by hand if they have a self-cleaning oven? A self-cleaning oven is so simple to operate and works wonderfully. Most simply require for you to turn them on and they just do their thing. The oven does get very hot while it is cleaning and there can be a strong smell. We simply go outdoors while ours is on to avoid the smell but still be close at hand.

With these 7 tips, your spring cleaning can go much faster. Now, I would love to know some ways that you make spring cleaning go faster. What can you share with me about that?
