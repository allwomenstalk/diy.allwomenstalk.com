18. Beach Party

Via Timeline Photos - <3 SWEET ...
If you can't get to the beach, bring the beach to your table!

***

Creating a beach-inspired tablescape is all about capturing the essence of the shore. Use sandy beige tablecloths paired with turquoise placemats to mimic the hues of beach waves. Scatter seashells and starfish across the table, and consider placing a small, clear bowl filled with sand and a votive candle at each setting. For a centerpiece, a rustic lantern surrounded by a wreath of driftwood can bring a touch of coastal charm right to your dinner party. Add string lights above to evoke the feeling of a beach bonfire night. These details will make your guests feel the warmth of the sun and the breeze of the sea without leaving the comfort of your home.
