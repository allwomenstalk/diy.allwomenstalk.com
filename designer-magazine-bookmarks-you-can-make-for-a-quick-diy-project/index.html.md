# Designer Magazine Bookmarks You Can Make for a Quick DIY Project ...

This is a super cute, easy, cheap and unique way to save your place!

## 1. Cute

The tutorial teaches using designer ads for the bookmarks to make them stylish, but really you can use any ad or article of your choice!

## 2. Easy

Can you paste and clip with scissors?

## 3. Cheap

Do you have a ton of magazines laying around the house? Don't throw away yet! Then, you can get a glue stick, poster board, ribbon, and one-hole punch at the dollar store!

## 4. Unique

These are "your" creations! Again, use any ad or article! Use whatever ribbon color!

Make these for yourself, loved ones, or any book lover you know.

Include these in books that you give as presents. A gift that keeps on giving!
