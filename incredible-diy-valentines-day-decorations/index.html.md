# 9 Incredible DIY Valentine's Day Crafts You Can Make in under an Hour ...

Whether you are decorating for a party or you are just trying to make your home festive, **DIY Valentine's Day decorations** are a must! These decorations are super easy to do, so even if you don't have a crafty thumb (like me!), you'll still be able put these awesome decorations together!

## 1. Heart Shaped Wall Art

![pink, flower, rose, petal, rose family,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ny/p4/efiwjkut5a68ec564fdc3596224528_600x800.jpg)

It's certainly fun to take down your usual wall art and replace it with some heart themed for Valentine's Day. This project is fast and fun and gives you something really great to hang around your house.
Tutorial: [tatertotsandjello.com](https://tatertotsandjello.com/2015/01/easy-10-minute-valentines-day-wall-art-free-templates.html#tCWC3hO3twwMc3Lt.32)

## 2. Cork Heart Decor

![finger food, snack, flavor,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ze/dt/k4nc5m0q5a68ec7b03c1f342604419_600x900.jpg)

If you have a bunch of wine corks lying around, use them to create this wonderful table decor. Combined with some flowers and candles and you have a setting that is romantic and attractive.
Tutorial: [sandandsisal.com](https://www.sandandsisal.com/2014/01/valentines-cork-heart.html)

## 3. Candy Flower Vase

![flower, flowering plant, pink, vase, cut flowers,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ji/qu/zezpgdks5a68ed3d1ccf4976049920_425x640.jpg)

How cute is this? You can scatter these easy to make flower arrangements all through your house as you celebrate Valentine's Day. Tulips are great, but roses or daisies also look fantastic.
Tutorial: [evolutionofstyleblog.com](https://www.evolutionofstyleblog.com/candy-and-flowers-for-valentines-day/)

## 4. Painted Mason Jars

![pink, mason jar, petal, floristry, drinkware,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o0/lp/ggvq982d5a68edc550fcb073567229_600x777.jpg)

You are going to have so much fun making these jars! In about an hour, you'll have some adorable pink and red decorations that you can use to liven up all the rooms in your house during the Valentine's Day season.

Tutorial: ‌ [masonjarcraftslove.com](https://masonjarcraftslove.com/valentine-heart-jars-painted-distressed/?crlt.pid=camp.aloPGJriPsK6%E2%80%8C)

## 5. Lovers in a Tree Wall Art﻿‌

![red, heart, love, product design, valentine's day,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n4/an/m4y884dt5a68ee1fb359d648886231_550x940.jpg)

Not only does this make a great piece of decor for your home, but it's also a fabulous gift idea. You can display it and make your boyfriend or husband smile at the same time. That's a Valentine's Day you'll never forget.
Tutorial: [thedatingdivas.com](https://www.thedatingdivas.com/two-little-lovers-decor/)

## 6. Hearts Galore Home Entry Decor

![pink, flower, petal, art, floristry,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ur/hs/rgr2xelk5a68ee7944ab8688911747_500x667.jpg)

Surprise anyone with this fun entry decor. Each heart can be printed with something you love about your kids, spouse or roommates. Or you can decorate them like conversation hearts. Either way, this is a really fun way to celebrate Valentine's Day.

Tutorial: ‌https://glutenfreerican.wordpress.com/2012/02/14/hearts-galore-2/?postpost=v2%E2%80%8C

## 7. Willow Wreath

![pink, flower, wreath, twig, decor,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ta/kx/e8cdb64j5a68eee7a7d6c423723428_444x640.jpg)

‌﻿Make your home a welcoming place this Valentine's Day with an easy to make wreath. The hearts really add a festive touch to the project and it will look fantastic hanging on your front door.
Tutorial: [thecardswedrew.com](https://thecardswedrew.com/valentines-heart-willow-wreath/)

## 8. Yarn Pom Pom Garland

![vase, centrepiece, sweetness, petal, tableware,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/se/fs/fjymj03z5a68ef31a15a7241039163_600x901.jpg)

These little yarn pom poms are addictive! They're so fast and easy to make and help you create a one-of-a-kind garland that you can hang anywhere in your house.
Tutorial: [mynameissnickerdoodle.com](https://www.mynameissnickerdoodle.com/valentine-pom-pom-garland/)

## 9. Circle of Love

![pink, petal, ribbon, peach, flower,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/as/fe/sos8cwnw5a68efd267e72818920585_500x676.jpg)

All you need to make these is some double sided scrapbook paper and ribbon. It's inexpensive, really cute and super fast. What more could you want from your Valentine's Day decor?

So girls, these are all of my DIY Valentine's Day decorations, do you have any that you make every year?
