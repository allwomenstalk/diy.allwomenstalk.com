# Here Are 30 Totally Easy Photo Frame Arrangements for Your House ...

Do you have a ton of photos you want to hang on the wall? Don't have any idea of how to get started? There's an art to getting all those frames arranged so they look good when you hang them up. Luckily, it isn't too hard to get what you want. Still need some inspiration? Check out these fantastic ideas and you'll be well on your way.

## 1. Use Picture Ledges

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a6/al/ui1yu_deions-family-playbook_furniture_room_chest-of-drawers_desk_395x580.jpg)

Via [Master Bedroom Picture Wall](https://thediyplaybook.com/diy-picture-ledge/) These ledges are a great idea because they make it easy to mix and match pictures whenever the mood strikes.

## 2. Wall Gallery

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ix/xi/k30m4_white_room_living-room_wall_home_427x640.jpg)

Frames in various sizes make your arrangement gallery worthy.

**Panel moulding** is a great way to frame a picture or create a larger space to showcase multiple pictures. The panel [moulding](https://mdfskirtingworld.co.uk/panel-moulding/) is an easy way to add a touch of elegance to a room and can really highlight the beauty of a particular picture or create a beautiful gallery wall. The panel moulding can be painted or stained to match any style and can be used in any room of the home. Whether you are looking for a statement frame for a single picture or an entire wall filled with art, **panel moulding** is an easy and stylish way to get the job done.
