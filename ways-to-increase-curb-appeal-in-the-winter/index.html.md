# 7 Ways to Increase Curb Appeal in the Winter ...

Curb appeal is important for your home to have no matter the season. You may want to have it in order to improve the look of your home or you may be trying to sell it. In either instance, curb appeal matters. It’s a lot easier to increase your curb appeal in the summer than it is in the winter but it can still be done. These are 7 ways you can do that.

## 1. Clean up the Leftovers of Summer

One way you can increase your curb appeal in the winter is to clean up the leftovers of summer. Get rid of the dead plants in your flower garden. Put your patio furniture away until next season. These little things can make a big difference. It shows that you take time to prepare for the changing seasons.

## 2. Get Rid of Outdoor Clutter

Additionally, outdoor clutter will take away from the appearance of your home. Take the time to make sure that everything’s neat and orderly on the outside of your home. This makes a big difference in the curb appeal. People always notice when a home looks tidy and well-kept. It makes a great first impression to anyone that comes to your home.

## 3. Put up a New Mailbox

It really is the little things that make a difference. Details like putting up a new mailbox if your old one is rusty or misshapen matter. People tend to notice mailboxes. It’s just one of the little things that can catch your eye. It doesn’t have to be a fancy mailbox; any mailbox that looks nice and neat is going to work fine.

## 4. Keep the Snow Shoveled

This one is important especially if you’re trying to sell your home. Keeping the snow shoveled shows that you keep your home properly cared for. If people have to wade through snow to get to the front door they’re going to be less likely to stop. Having a freshly shoveled sidewalk is inviting to prospective buyers. It makes them want to see the inside of your home.

## 5. Paint Your Door a Colorful Hue

Let’s face it. Winter is a dreary and gray time of year. Painting your door a new color can really brighten things up. Aqua, green or even burgundy can all be good color choices. Those are all colors that can really pop against the bright white of snow.

## 6. Hang a Pretty Wreath on Your Door

Hanging a pretty wreath on your front door is the perfect way to dress it up. It’s also the finishing touch to a newly painted door. It shows pride in your home and will welcome your guests in. A pretty wreath helps to make a great first impression. There are many beautiful wreaths to choose from, whether you decide to make your own or buy one. I found instructions for a beautiful burlap wreath on Pinterest that I plan to make for our home’s front door.

## 7. Buy a New Welcome Mat

This is such a small thing but it makes a big impact. Buying a new welcome mat is a nice touch to add curb appeal. It truly does add a welcoming look to visitors. You can pick one up practically anywhere. Even dollar stores carry them. Just purchase one you like on your next stop to pick up home necessities.

These are 7 things you can do to improve the curb appeal of your home in the winter. What ideas do you have? I’d love to hear them!
