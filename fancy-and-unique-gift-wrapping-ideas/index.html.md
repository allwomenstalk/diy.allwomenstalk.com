# 49 Fancy and Unique Gift Wrapping Ideas ...

Sure, you can wrap a package with paper and a bow, but if you're looking for gift wrapping ideas that go above and beyond, you're going to love what you see here. It's great to give someone a gift that is just as beautiful on the outside as it is on the inside, so here are some gift wrapping ideas to get you started.

## 1. Great for Kids

![www,lines,across,com,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/op/mb/skenc_www_lines_across_com_458x640.jpg)

Source: [Gift Wrapping Ideas: 6 Creative ...](https://www.craftfoxes.com/blog/gift-wrapping-ideas-6-distinctive-ways-to-wrap-packages)
A package that your child can color while he waits to open gifts? I say that's one of the best gift wrapping ideas there is.

## 2. Simple and Elegant

![box,label,brand,wood,gift,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d7/rw/li4p9_box_label_brand_wood_gift_500x680.jpg)

Source: [linprobable.tumblr.com](https://linprobable.tumblr.com/post/67877849317)
These simple square tags with a single sprig of greenery are perfect for a fancy Christmas party.

## 3. Chalkboard Paper

![christmas decoration,To:,Dawid,From,Bouie,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mw/y9/xzvgo_christmas-decoration_to_dawid_from_bouie_500x517.jpg)

Source: [christmas 'chalkboard' packaging | going ...](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.goinghometoroost.com/2012/christmas/christmas-chalkboard-packaging/)
This paper is fun because you can customize it any way you want to. Kids will love it!

## 4. Love Letter

_ Source: bloglovin.com_
Pink paper or written sentiments in pink are perfect for a gift you're giving your significant other. The brown contrasts perfectly.

## 5. A Little Girly

![textile,material,paper,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pt/g5/eqzxb_textile_material_paper_480x640.jpg)

## 6. Animals

![textile,ceramic,label,material,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ge/xu/xp8q7_textile_ceramic_label_material_500x574.jpg)
