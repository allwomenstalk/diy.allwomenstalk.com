# DIY Coffee Cup Goodie Gift ...

Easter is coming up! Why not make gifts to give that were thought of around Christmas? In this video, learn **how to make a coffee cup goodie gift**!

The preparations may all be purchased at the... drum roll please... Dollar store!

You do not have to use Christmas colors ... use some pink maybe.

You do not have to use Christmas cookies ... use some goodies sold in the month of April or make your own ... Maybe choose a cupcake instead?

Instead of using hot chocolate, why not some coffee?

Make it your own! What would YOU like to receive? They always say to make something that you would like to receive yourself.

I hope you like this video and that it gives you some inspiration for what to give to loved ones, friends, co-workers, etc. this Easter.
