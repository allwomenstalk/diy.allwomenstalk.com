# Coffee Filters: Not Just for Coffee!

This DIY is so adorable! The tutorial shows how to make coffee filter wreaths! The most you are going to spend is on the hot glue! Follow along!

Searching through YouTube to find interesting videos for you is really exciting. I do have to go through a lot of duds to find the hidden diamonds. I like unique. I like cheap. I like useful.

With coffee filters and paper plates ringing in at a buck for a buck a package at the dollar store, you can make a whole lot of these coffee filter wreaths.

Just because they are white does not mean that they are strictly for the winter season. Place colorful ribbons and bows on it to celebrate different occasions. Do you have some ribbon at home just taking up room?

I hope you like the video! Happy crafting!
