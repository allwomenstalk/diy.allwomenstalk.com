13 Go up with a Ladder

Source: Tight for Space for a

***

Ingenious and chic—utilize a slender, decorative ladder to hold the bedside essentials aloft. This innovative approach maximizes vertical space, making it perfect for pint-sized rooms that can't accommodate traditional furniture. Simply lean the ladder against the wall and use the rungs to hang, drape, or place items like books, glasses, or even a small plant. Not only does this save precious floor space, but it also adds a touch of bohemian flair to the room, blending functionality with style. And the best bit? It's a breeze to move around, giving you the flexibility to switch up your decor with ease.
