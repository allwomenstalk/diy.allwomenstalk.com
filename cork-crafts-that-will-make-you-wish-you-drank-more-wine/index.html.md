# 39 Genius DIY Cork Projects That Will Make You Wish You Drank More Wine

With the holiday season right around the corner, you know the wine will be flowing a bit more freely—making now the absolute best time to explore creative diy cork projects. Whether you stockpile corks throughout the year or have been saving them for a rainy day, that craft day has finally arrived. Cork is fantastic in home decoration because of its warm texture, natural durability, and eco-friendly upcycling potential. If you are looking for clever wine cork ideas to turn your collection into functional art or thoughtful handmade gifts, you are in the right place. Ready to be inspired by a multitude of creative crafts using corks?
