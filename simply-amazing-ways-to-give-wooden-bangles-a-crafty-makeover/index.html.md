# 8 Simply Amazing Ways to Give Wooden Bangles a Crafty Makeover ...

Any true-blue fashionista has a stack of wooden bangles on her dresser. But if you want something truly unique and beautiful, we suggest giving wooden bangles a crafty makeover. For fantastic ideas, check out the DIYs below. They're cute and are sure to make your wrist bling adventures extra fun.

## 1. Cover Them with Fabric

![Cover Them with Fabric](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ce/ku/e9pl8ai8550ebaa6b76f8496054293_600x400.jpg)

These DIY wooden bangles are all sorts of pretty! They are quite easy to make as well. You simply cut strips of beautiful fabric and then Mod Podge them around the wooden bangles. Let dry and wear to complete your favorite outfit. Make sure to use fabric strips with lovely prints.

Source: [sarahhearts.com](https://www.sarahhearts.com/fabric-bangles/)

## 2. Add Pretty Pennant Flags

![Add Pretty Pennant Flags](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nn/f9/epq676hb550ebaa74582f543541829_600x400.jpg)

How cute is this DIY wooden bangle? This particular project calls for the use of Mod Podge as well. But instead of fabric strips, you will be adding paper pennant flags onto the surface of the wooden bangle. Before you do that, though, you will want to paint the bangle first. Use a color that will complement the prints or hues of your paper flags.

Source: [modpodgerocksblog.com](https://modpodgerocksblog.com/how-to-make-bangle-bracelets/)

## 3. Glue Vintage Accessories

![Glue Vintage Accessories](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/px/yk/hzoqk1so550ebaa7afe77661016500_600x400.jpg)

Got old pieces of jewelry that have seen better days? Disassemble some of them and then use your favorite parts to give wooden bangles a sparkly makeover. You will need only industrial glue for this project. E600 should work perfectly.

Source: [swingingonastarcreations.blogspot.com](http://swingingonastarcreations.blogspot.com/2011/10/if-you-cant-afford-latest-trends-maybe.html)

## 4. Knit over Them

![Knit over Them](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ge/c3/xjiwzjqz550ebaa86b1ba968455208_600x570.jpg)

Doesn't this DIY wooden bangle look cozy? For those who feel that a wooden accessory is too hard on the wrist bone, adding a cozy knitted cover is just the ticket. Putting a cute heart bead is optional but it's a detail that we feel should not be skipped.

Source: [bloglaunchfemininetouch.blogspot.co.uk](http://bloglaunchfemininetouch.blogspot.com/2013/12/free-tutorial-knitted-childs-bracelet.html)

## 5. Wrap with Ribbon

![Wrap with Ribbon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cc/p5/bs78iin8550ebaa9198f9133887646_600x450.jpg)

This is one lush bangle. It is the perfect combination of sexy and stunning. The great thing about this fun jewelry DIY is that you can go about it in so many ways and the end results will range from cute to ultra hot. You can choose plain ribbon in dark colors or go for printed material. The possibilities are endless.

Source: [confettiworkshop.blogspot.com](http://confettiworkshop.blogspot.com/2014/01/diy-ribbon-wrapped-bangle.html)

## 6. Stencil a Pattern

![Stencil a Pattern](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mo/ij/ttfd8kp5550ebaa98484b970083669_600x540.jpg)

This wooden bangle, as you can see has a flat surface. This makes working with a stencil easier. But if your stencil is pliant enough or you cut a pattern especially for your wooden bangle, I think a dome and round bangle type will work as well. After you let your stenciled design dry, add a coat of Mod Podge to protect your new piece of jewelry.

Source: [oneartsymama.com](https://www.amylattacreations.com/2014/01/stenciled-bangle.html)

## 7. Paint Them

![Paint Them](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/to/h5/en2cfru0550ebaaa0a71f444835941_600x400.jpg)

This is the easiest ways to craft with plain wooden bangles. In fact, when you think of wooden bangle projects, you likely immediately consider painting a design over the surface. Check out the link below for tips on how to go about painting a pattern and protecting your finished item with gloss.

Source: [sarahhearts.com](https://www.sarahhearts.com/painted-wood-bangles/)

## 8. Cover with Yarn

![Cover with Yarn](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ea/e6/p7ppua57550ebaaaf2d52290785433_600x398.jpg)

This is similar to the ribbon wooden bangle DIY but it will not call for a bow finish. Basically, you wrap yarn or rattail cord around a wooden bangle until the whole thing is covered. To finish, you snip the yarn or cord along the inner section and secure with glue.

Source: [loveumadly.com](https://goo.gl/RGBdfQ)

When you step into your local craft store this week, don't forget to grab plain wooden bangles. Re-read the tutorials above and give your plain accessories a fun and stylish facelift.
