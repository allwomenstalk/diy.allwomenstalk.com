# 24 Reusable Bags That Will Totally Change Your Shopping Habits ...

Are you conscious of your carbon footprint? Do you want to do what you can to make the world a better place for future generations? Me too! One way you can do that is to take your own reusable bags to the supermarket instead of using those plastic ones they offer you there. All those plastic bags clog up landfills and require a tone of energy to produce. Making your own bags is pretty easy to do and then you can take them with you each time you shop. Here are some fun ideas to inspire you.
