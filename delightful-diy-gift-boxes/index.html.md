# 10 Delightful DIY Gift Boxes ...

If you're still stressing about last minute gifts, we are here to assist you in the form of DIY Gift Boxes. Not only are these **DIY gift boxes** useful, they will also calm your nerves. If, like me, you find doing or making something with your hands a cure to stress, then I suggest that you give these **DIY gift boxes** a go. Happy Christmas crafting!

## 1. House

![House](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/12/18_500x375.jpg)

How cute! Do you have new neighbors? Or do you have a friend who loves to make dollhouses? If you answered yes, this house-themed **DIY gift box** is perfect for those treats you're planning to give said neighbors or friend.

[Tutorial](https://goo.gl/uk7fxr)

## 2. Folded

![Folded](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/12/28-560x448_560x448.jpg)

This particular **DIY gift box** idea is clever and cute. If you happen to have lots of pretty card stock, you can use this tutorial to make pretty gift packaging. With a few simple folding moves and pretty embellishments, your gift will look absolutely gorgeous.

[Tutorial](https://cosmocricket.typepad.com/cosmo_cricket/2009/09/easy-folded-gift-boxes.html)

## 3. Book Pages

![Book Pages](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/12/38_550x378.jpg)

These are cardboard boxes covered with book pages. Pretty, right? I love this idea because you can design it in so many ways. You might want to refrain from using Christmas wrapping paper, though, so that your recipient can use/display the lovely box all year.

[Tutorial](http://craftandcreativity.com/blog/2012/10/31/morebookrecycling/)

## 4. Fabric

![Fabric](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/12/47_356x301.jpg)

The method used for making these **DIY gift boxes** is similar to the previous craft tutorial but instead of using book pages, you will be covering cardboard boxes with fabric.

[Tutorial](https://www.familyhandyman.com/)

## 5. Pyramid

_ This is a unique git box that is sure to delight. In fact, if you are doubtful about your gift, I suggest that you consider making this pyramid** DIY gift box** to distract your recipient.

Tutorial_

## 6. Flower Top

_ How cute! The tutorial link below has a template that you can download and print. For this craft project, you will only need the template, scissors, card stock, and double-sided tape. Cut the template and fold according to the instructions. Place gift inside and you're done.

Tutorial_

## 7. Film Canister

![Film Canister](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/12/77-560x420_560x420.jpg)

This is a great gift box idea for tiny presents, such as jewelry or cash. Not only is this a clever way to wrap small gifts, these prettified film canisters can also serve as tree ornaments. Very clever.

[Tutorial](https://www.momtastic.com/family/diy-for-moms/170827-diy-film-canister-gift-box-ornament/)

## 8. Cupcake

_ Adorable! These gift boxes are sure to be as treasured as the stuff inside them. Check out Heidi's post to learn how to make different kinds of "cupcake frosting" for your round gift box. This idea is so awesome you might find yourself making more than you need.

Tutorial_

## 9. Twist Snowflake

![Twist Snowflake](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/12/97_547x443.jpg)

This is similar to the flower top idea but this snowflake **DIY gift box** has a more holiday look. White card stock works for this craft project but printed paper will work, too.

[Tutorial](https://www.extremepapercrafting.com/2010/02/twist-top-snowflake-box.html)

## 10. Tree

![Tree](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/12/106_450x320.jpg)

Won't it be cool if the gifts under your tree are in the shape of mini-trees? Oh if only we can magically shrink gifts so that they will fit inside these adorable tree boxes.

[Tutorial](http://www.danielledaws.com/2010/08/christmas-tree-box-tutorial.html)

So there you have cute **DIY gift boxes**. If you want your gifts to make an impact, these **DIY gift boxes** are here to help. Enjoy!
