# 17 Really Cool K Cup Things You Can Make ...

At my house, everyone loves the Keurig machine, so I always have fun finding new K cup things. There are fun ways to organize your pods and a load of really cool things you can do with them when they are empty. My favorite thing is how many tasty options there are that you can make with the Keurig. I can spend quite a bit of time on the coffee aisle looking at all the choices. So, I'm going to give you some examples of fun K cup things you can do. Let me know how it goes!

## 6. Turn the Cups into Seed Starters

![Turn the Cups into Seed Starters](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ww/z4/xxnhh0lf_600x488.jpg)

Via [Fresh Eggs Daily](https://www.fresheggsdaily.blog/2014/02/k-cup-recycling-toss-grounds-in-your.html)
Once your K cup pods are used, you can empty and rinse them, then use them to start your garden seeds. Simply add a bit of potting soil, toss in your seeds and give them a bit of water. Once they sprout, you can move them into your outdoor garden space.
