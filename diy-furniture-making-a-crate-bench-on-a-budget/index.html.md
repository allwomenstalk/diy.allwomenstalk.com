# DIY Furniture: Making a Crate Bench on a Budget ...

This bench would be perfect to have by a window, whether to daydream, read, etc. The little lady in this video shows you how, cheaply and easily.

She reminds me of myself, getting a bargain whenever possible! If you are a regular reader of my reviews, I like to save money, yet still have a quality product.

Simply, buy two crates, a board and some glue. I would not even paint, leaving a fresh wood look. But, you might want a more polished look with white, pink, etc. I would also want a nice pillow and a throw.

A really cute DIY. A nice gift to give yourself, a relative or a friend.
