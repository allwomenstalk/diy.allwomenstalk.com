# Send These Diy Thank You Notes out after the Holidays ...

I bet you got some awesome gifts for Christmas this year, didn't you? And you probably want to thank the people who gave them to you with a heartfelt note of gratitude. A handmade gift adds more personality to the card and gives you a fun project to tackle once the decorations and ornaments are all packed back up. Everyone is going to love getting one of these DIY thank you cards.
