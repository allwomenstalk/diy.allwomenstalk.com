### Quick Tips for DIY Success

- Always prep your surfaces before painting or gluing for better longevity.

- Shop at local thrift stores for unique bases for your projects.

- Don't be afraid to mix textures like lace, rope, and metallic finishes.

- Invest in a good quality [hot glue gun](https://www.michaels.com/) for various crafting needs.
