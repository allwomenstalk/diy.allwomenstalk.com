# Gorgeous Collage Walls That'll Make Your Home Feel Even Homier ...

Collage walls are such an easy way to make boring walls in your house a lot more fun! If you're looking for ways to decorate but can't find any artwork that you're obsessed with, turn a wall into a collage wall. There are so many different designs and ideas you can do, so if you're looking for inspiration, look no further than this list!

## 1. Family Wall

![Family Wall](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/go/pt/xy2md0uq567ac6e38afb8787371607_600x600.jpg)

If family is the center of your life, let that be reflected as the center of your family with this collage wall!

## 2. Different Colors and Textures

![Different Colors and Textures](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q5/z9/p903sw04567166a0cc8f0445063305_600x940.jpg)

The contrast of different colors and textures works so perfectly in this room!

## 3. Perfectly Spaced Wall

![Perfectly Spaced Wall](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mw/o1/rgx1u6u6567166a0efb6e399957207_600x766.jpg)

Don't you love the spacing of this gallery wall? So pretty!

## 4. Front Entryway Wall

![Front Entryway Wall](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ih/q5/sbni4i2v567166a0c611a208488654_590x885.jpg)

Turn a boring entryway wall into a masterpiece with a collage wall like this!

## 5. Buffet Table Collage

![Buffet Table Collage](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j8/k7/unw7z2qd567166a0a86a1217777141_450x450.jpg)

If your buffet table is looking lonely, decorate it with this cute collage wall idea.

## 6. Mirrored Table and Pastels

![Mirrored Table and Pastels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ww/xv/uy5xmxtf567166a122355797340508_600x900.jpg)

## 7. Boy's Room

![Boy's Room](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jc/xw/iuvzpntc567166a187255481933871_540x810.jpg)

Boy's rooms don't have to be boring! Make them fun and creative with a bright collage wall!

## 8. Dark Wood

![Dark Wood](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dn/ex/kv20mqto567ac6e4290e5617688830_600x903.jpg)

This dark wood looks so great as a collage wall!

## 9. Stairway Collage

Stairway collages are one of my all-time favorite classic decorating moves, and this look is no exception!

## 10. Entryway Canvases

![Entryway Canvases](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zb/l2/spqw5dyu567166a21c56b226988677_481x720.jpg)

## 11. Family Room Canvases

![Family Room Canvases](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y3/x5/pw9o82fm567ac73615882355820948_600x619.jpg)

These black and white canvases are perfect for a family room!

## 12. White Accents

![White Accents](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hp/yc/zomqu96r567166a26b93a811285539_439x439.jpg)

## 13. Corner Collage

![Corner Collage](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ut/p5/vm1wspab567ac6e48f701831239526_600x780.jpg)

## 14. Blue Frame

![Blue Frame](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jc/n6/fw4kh6qi567166a3b7c3c568056457_600x904.jpg)

This light blue frame is such a great addition to a collage wall!

## 15. Framed Collage Wall

![Framed Collage Wall](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fv/ii/uksmpkfk567166a40dad1937472746_600x1066.jpg)

Frame everything, even things that aren't pictures, for an adorable and uniform look.

## 16. Photobooth Pictures

![Photobooth Pictures](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c7/so/ezdmuavu567166a3626a1319430521_456x600.jpg)

Turn photobooth pictures into works of art with this idea!

## 17. Nursery Collage

![Nursery Collage](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t2/li/ijcyzb5z567166a3ad735144504109_550x745.jpg)

Bring color into a nursery with this cute changing table collage!

## 18. Ledge Collage

![Ledge Collage](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ev/p2/njrnvyqa567166a3bb538848658921_422x634.jpg)

Save time by layering art on a ledge rather than hanging it up. It's a chic look and will save you hours of time!

## 19. Letters

![Letters](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vc/g0/wtj6tf57567166a501715430548844_600x891.jpg)

Make a collage wall out of letters of your first name rather than pictures! So cute!

## 20. Clock Collage Wall

![Clock Collage Wall](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c1/sp/wn7fcda4567166a4837b8211193854_480x640.jpg)

Sometimes a clock collage wall just works better than a picture collage wall, and this is a perfect example of that!

## 21. Photo Collage Wall

![Photo Collage Wall](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oi/uu/px7eywr0567166a56cb51586633139_600x945.jpg)

Take small pictures and hang them in big frames for a dramatic but cute look!

## 22. Hooks and Pictures

![Hooks and Pictures](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sh/vi/hp3zjz9w567ac6e4b4273313571587_600x900.jpg)
