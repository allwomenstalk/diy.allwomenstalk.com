# 22 Diy Lampshades You Can Make at Home ...

A great lampshade can make any room in your house look just the way you want it to. Unfortunately, it can be hard to find exactly what you want in stores. That's why DIY lampshades are so perfect. You can modify an inexpensive lampshade so that it looks exactly the way you need it to for your space. Here are some fabulous, and easy ways, to create the masterpiece you need to deck out your living room, bedroom or office. Get ready to be impressed!

## 1. Coat the inside of a Lampshade with Glitter

![Coat the inside of a Lampshade with Glitter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dg/pw/z2no90vt54da37a21e75f070196216_600x450.jpg)

Via [31 Home Decor Hacks That ...](https://www.buzzfeed.com/peggy/cheap-and-easy-decorating-hacks-that-are-borderline-geniu?sub=2601289_1629733)
