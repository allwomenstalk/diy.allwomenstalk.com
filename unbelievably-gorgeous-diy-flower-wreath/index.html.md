# Unbelievably Gorgeous DIY Flower Wreath ...

Tracy, of Gift Basket Appeal, gives a tutorial that is so so cute. Buy your favorite flowers and greenery for a floral feeling that you love, whether for your house, a loved one or a friend.

All it takes is a foam wreath form, a pair of pliers, flowers, greenery, and a small piece of ribbon. By following the video along, if you don't do crafts already, you can now! Not hard, huh?

Make up different colors. Maybe make some up to coordinate with the time of year.

At a buck each for the supplies, you can create a masterpiece that looks like you bought it at a craft store made by a master crafter.
