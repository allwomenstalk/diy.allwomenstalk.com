# 10 Rose Gold Room Decor Ideas for Girls Who Love a Simple Kind of Pretty ...

Looking for a way to transform your room into something chic? **Rose gold decor ideas** are a great idea. Being the newest color in the fancy world of today, rose gold is giving new life to home decors and trendy accessories.

Instead of looking for the best products, below are the most fascinating pieces, each of which will leave you surprised in a good way. The best way to use rose gold is with white. It gives you a soothing aura with a fancy homey environment.
Grab a look at these **ways to add rose gold to your decor.**

## 1. Fancy Mirror

![furniture, interior design, product design, wood, window,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ox/zs/uqua55f859ac61705fda2275945962_523x523.jpg)

A rose gold mirror for decor can be one of the most stylish ways to decorate a room. Apart from looking elegant, it is also pulling all the attention into the room. A fancy mirror is one of the best **rose gold decor ideas.**

## 2. Coasters

![wood, product design, floor, table, flooring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lh/fc/vhirvv1z59ac61fd185e4067495126_504x504.jpg)

Coasters are a mini product that’ll compliment the rose gold interior of your room. Instead of buying some old wooden coasters, why not pick up a more elegant choice?

## 3. Hanging Lamps

![furniture, interior design, home, table, product design,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s4/gu/kql5q8i059ac62260b3ec624687182_600x600.jpg)

Lamps are the perfect way to lighten up an entire room. Hanging lamps not only look lavish but are also a great space saver. When side table lamps take up a lot of space on your table, hanging lamps solve the problem with style.

## 4. Clock

![font, clock, product design, alarm clock,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lb/g8/ft81f59b59ac624fa8c78529914751_600x600.jpg)

Clocks are a must-have, and a unique rose color with a pinch of marble looks amazingly perfect in most rooms of your house. No matter the shape or size, you'll love seeing a rose gold clock every time you enter the room.

## 5. Nature

![flower, vase, cut flowers, flower arranging, floristry,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ka/q5/xw3lkgo659ac62751c014440721939_600x600.jpg)

Plants make for great home decor, giving fresh ambiance and to the room. Put your plant into a rose gold pot for impact and beauty in your home.

## 6. Lights

![copper, lighting, metal, jewellery, light fixture,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t9/vg/w11h4wcw59ac62b5e4ddc433071423_600x600.jpg)

Light up your room with rose gold lights that make every corner of your room look attractive and soothing. You could place the lights around the mirror, above your bed, or simply make them a wall hanging. Get creative, and find the best space for the lights to make your room glow!

## 7. Pillow Cover

![cushion, pillow, heart, throw pillow,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dp/zz/g9snrrow59ac6308daf68226654629_600x582.jpg)

A bed with a lot of vibrant colored pillows makes your room look catchy and sharp. Rose gold is noticeable and is one of the simplest ways to transform your room in one go.

## 8. Cabinet Hardware

![metal, product design, product, beige,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wd/il/jc1cmsqc59ac639f136c1640033723_584x584.jpg)

Maybe it is time to get new hardware to make your furniture look like new and cabinets. It not only changes your furniture, but also makes your room look better than ever!

## 9. Rose Gold Bed

![bed frame, furniture, product, bed, shelf,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fq/ip/yq4nmzx059ac63c302314029427789_600x600.jpg)

A unique color that glows in itself is a must-have and you can truly showcase it with your bed!

## 10. Book Holder

![product, product design, box, product, packaging and labeling,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/at/oh/brqduc4e59ac63f3e44ea043819605_410x410.jpg)
