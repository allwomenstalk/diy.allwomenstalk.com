# Some of the Best Repurpose Projects I Have Seen: 27 Things to do with Old Tires ...

I'm enamored with the repurposing of old tires for two main reasons. One is that you produce something very unusual (and hopefully useful or beautifui) and two, it keeps at least some tires out of landfill. When you see some of the great things you can make out of old tires, you'll agree with at last one of my reasons, if not both.
