# 10 Brilliant DIY Bat Halloween Crafts ...

We continue our fun-tober extravaganza with these DIY Bat Halloween Crafts. We have here craft projects that are a perfect mix of adorable and creepy. From simple **bat Halloween DIYs** to some that are a little bit complicated, these craft projects are sure to tickle your creepy crafting fancy. Enjoy!

## 2. Applique

![Applique](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/10/27-560x431_560x431.jpg)

I find this **DIY bat Halloween decor** idea incredibly cute, too. I love the fact these applique bats look like they're attacking the pristine white pillow. According to HGTV, this pillow "requires basic sewing skills." Perfect!

[Tutorial](https://www.hgtv.com/design/make-and-celebrate/handmade/how-to-make-a-halloween-applique-pillow)

## 3. Treat Bucket

![Treat Bucket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/10/36_300x250.jpg)

Here is a **bat-inspired craft project** for your little one. A treat bucket like this will surely add an undeniably cute element to your kid's Halloween get-up. These bat buckets would also make for displaying treats at your party easier.

[Tutorial](https://www.parents.com/holiday/halloween/crafts/bat-trick-or-treat-bucket/)

## 4. Garland

![Garland](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/10/47-560x418_560x418.jpg)

How cool! This is my favorite from this list of **Halloween bat-inspired crafts.** To make this, you will need card stock, printer, scissors, hole punch, and wire. You print the bat silhouettes, connect them using wire, and hang!

[Tutorial](https://brasspaperclip.typepad.com/brass_paperclip/2011/10/diy-halloween-bat-garland.html)

## 5. Mobile

![Mobile](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/10/57_427x312.jpg)

What's a Halloween decor list without mentioning a bat mobile? No, not THAT bat mobile. To make one, you will be needing a branch or twig spray painted in black and paper bats. Check out the link for the perfect way to hang this Halloween mobile.

[Tutorial](http://rebeccasdiy.blogspot.com/2011/10/halloween-diy-fladdermusmobil.html)

## 6. Wall Art

![Wall Art](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/10/67-560x380_560x380.jpg)

One simple way to decorate with something bat-inspired this Halloween is to make wall art. The idea after the link is brought to us by the ladies of eighteen25. What they did was stencil a canvas with a Martha Stewart stencil and paint a bat silhouette over the pattern. Easy!

[Inspiration](http://eighteen25.blogspot.com/2011/08/new-martha-stewart-decorative-paint.html)

## 7. Doormat

![Doormat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/10/77_400x246.jpg)

A great way to welcome guests to your home this month! To make this, you will need an outdoor rug, a stencil of a bat, scissors, masking tape, and spray paint. You simply tape the stencil to the rug and spray paint. That's it.

[Tutorial](https://goo.gl/Emx97X)

## 9. Napkin Rings

![Napkin Rings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/10/94_300x250.jpg)

Here we have another **bat-themed Halloween idea** for that party you're planning. This is a fairly simple craft project that will require only tracing paper, pencil, black construction paper, and scissors. Check out the link for the tutorial.

[Tutorial](https://www.womansday.com/home/crafts-projects/how-to/a5996/halloween-craft-how-to-bat-napkin-rings-123823/)

## 10. Bat-O-Lantern

![Bat-O-Lantern](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/10/104_420x344.jpg)

Look at these adorable pumpkins! To make these, you will spray paint the pumpkins black and adhere the bat parts to the pumpkin using glue or toothpicks. The tutorial after the link has downloadable bat patterns for your crafting pleasure.

[Tutorial](https://goo.gl/nSfd2t)
