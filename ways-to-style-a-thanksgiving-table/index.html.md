# Stunning Thanksgiving Table Inspo for Girls Looking for Fresh Decor Ideas ...

It's one of the annual celebrations that calls for special decorations so if you're looking for ways to style a Thanksgiving table, I've got a great selection to inspire you. Making use of all the symbols of the fall season, these ways to style a Thanksgiving table will make your meal an occasion to remember.

## 1. Faux Pumpkins

![Faux Pumpkins](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b8/su/v8ez32is_600x901.jpg)

If you're clever, some ways to style a Thanksgiving table can last throughout fall. Like these faux pumpkins.

## 2. Metallic Pumpkins

![Metallic Pumpkins](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i6/dk/lwoewesi_550x733.jpg)

Gold pumpkins will add glam to your table.

## 3. Autumn Leaves Runner

![Autumn Leaves Runner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/df/rb/ylkfdwor581c7cbf40c5a385780038_500x750.jpg)

Use multicolored autumn leaves and clear cellophane to make a table runner that is not only gorgeous but functional as well!

## 4. Branches and Flowers

![Branches and Flowers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l1/jf/bq1z6xg2581c7cd4d0f26360594423_500x666.jpg)

Using branches from your backyard, attach them to an empty coffee can with hot glue, then fill with flowers.

## 5. Thanksgiving Place Cards

![Thanksgiving Place Cards](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rh/qg/lklc1b65_600x799.jpg)

Simplicity that combines rustic charm with elegance.

## 6. Pumpkin Centerpiece

![Pumpkin Centerpiece](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v4/x7/xiqft1jd_570x854.jpg)

Pumpkins and burlap are a good mix.

## 7. Wine Glasses

![Wine Glasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mq/oj/tu9a5a0e581c7cedad77e281412428_500x738.jpg)

If you're on a budget, there are still easy but chic ways to style a Thanksgiving table.

## 8. Wheat Centerpiece

![Wheat Centerpiece](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z2/nw/ha20cr7r_550x825.jpg)

If you can get your hands on a sheaf of wheat, this is a great idea.

## 9. Planter Box

![Planter Box](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fq/hn/dzpeiwes_600x902.jpg)

You know that old wooden box you've had lying around for simply ages?

## 10. Kraft Paper

![Kraft Paper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iq/j9/nhcf5beq581c7d2c6b163235942208_500x638.jpg)

Now this is what you call super budget-friendly! Draw place settings on a kraft paper tablecloth and stick some fruit in pots!

## 11. Chocolate Brown

![Chocolate Brown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wq/iz/qb2df081_600x800.jpg)

There's no rule that says you can't choose a different color to the traditional.

## 12. Organic

![Organic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xt/wg/tr6ovtfr_600x800.jpg)

Make a runner from twigs and scatter some leaves.

## 13. The Kids Table

![The Kids Table](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/al/t3/r5k4urbz_528x650.jpg)

Get the kids involved in making the decorations for their table.

## 14. Napkins

![Napkins](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pe/ij/wynuumbx_450x600.jpg)

Fancy napkins are an easy way to jzoosh up your table. Check out ideas here [25 Tutorials for How to Fold Napkins ... @Neecey](https://food.allwomenstalk.com/tutorials-for-how-to-fold-napkins/)

## 15. Linen, Rosemary and Twine

![Linen, Rosemary and Twine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zl/so/smo4k4fb_600x900.jpg)

Simple but pretty.

## 16. Nutty Condiments

![Nutty Condiments](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k2/hv/b22ewmuk_600x800.jpg)

Halve a walnut and use the shells to fill with salt & pepper. Each person gets one at their place setting and you won't need to keep passing condiments around.

## 17. Paper Pumpkins

![Paper Pumpkins](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/at/c8/jdrlxag2581c7d4f5cca6734922225_500x666.jpg)

Handy at crafts? Get out your paper and scissors.

## 18. 5 Minute Centerpiece

![5 Minute Centerpiece](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gf/wy/o64uzzvy581c7d6aa1214857894677_500x666.jpg)

Put a hurricane vase with candle in a large bowl and surround with pine cones, fruits and mini pumpkins.

## 19. String of Blessings

![String of Blessings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fg/vh/rf6uca19_400x400.jpg)

Print out individual words on tabs, and fold them around twine. Weave the twine along the center of the table and around the place settings.

## 20. Pumpkin Bling

![Pumpkin Bling](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t6/p2/tgcevvnd_600x900.jpg)

Go with a gold or silver theme.

## 21. Fall Centerpiece

![Fall Centerpiece](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h4/bk/f95x4jdw_540x1000.jpg)

Fill a vase with cranberries and then fresh flowers.

## 22. Pumpkin Jars

![Pumpkin Jars](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wt/rz/yx1ed0i6_427x640.jpg)

Buy faux pumpkins from the hobby store and use them year after year.

## 23. Assorted Pumpkins

![Assorted Pumpkins](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ro/sz/iqs2nfx2_560x793.jpg)

Make a centerpiece of interesting pumpkins and gourds interspersed with leaves and flowers.

## 24. Pumpkin and Gourd Candles

![Pumpkin and Gourd Candles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ze/nl/o8di2pmi581c7da29da89913937337_500x537.jpg)

Cut out enough flesh to make room for a candle.

## 25. Peas and Beans

![Peas and Beans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qo/l0/lo2ujprb_400x635.jpg)

Use different beans, seeds and corn in layers.

## 26. Rosemary Wreath Napkin Holders

![Rosemary Wreath Napkin Holders](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bt/j6/do9tn4hl_600x799.jpg)

Festive and cheap.

## 27. DIY Thanksgiving Table Runner

![DIY Thanksgiving Table Runner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iz/ab/z7trx6wx_507x507.jpg)

What a great family tradition to start this year.

## 28. Acorns and Flowers

![Acorns and Flowers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nc/re/vqumeb8g_450x672.jpg)

## 29. Wheat and Cranberries
