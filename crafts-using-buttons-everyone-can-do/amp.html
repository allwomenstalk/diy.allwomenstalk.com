3 Button Hairpins

Via Button Hairpins

It's a cinch to give bobby pins a makeover.

***

Button hairpins are an easy and fun way to add a unique touch to your hair styling. With just a few items and a few minutes of your time, you can create beautiful hair accessories. All you need is some buttons, bobby pins, and a hot glue gun.

Start by selecting the buttons you want to use. You can either use a single large button or several smaller ones. Then, apply a dab of hot glue to the back of the button and attach it to the bobby pin. Allow the glue to cool and harden before moving on to the next pin.

Once all of your pins are complete, you can style your hair in any way you like. Whether you choose to wear your hair up or down, the button hairpins will add a unique and stylish touch. You can also use the pins to create a variety of looks, such as a half-up style, a side braid, or a messy bun.

Button hairpins can also be used to create beautiful jewelry. Simply attach the pins to a chain or cord to make a necklace or bracelet. You can also make earrings by attaching the pins to earring posts.
