# 7 Creative Ombre Craft Projects You Need to Try

If you thought the ombre look was just a hair trend, wait until you see these amazing ombre craft ideas. From home and holiday decor to clothing and accessories, you are going to love these tutorials. These ideas are fun to make with your kids or your friends, and there is something for everyone. There are some things that the ombre look just does not work on, so avoid trying this trend on anything you can get your hands on. Just stick to the list and if it does not work out, do not blame me, simply say it was a Pinterest fail! For more great inspiration, you might enjoy our favorite [craft ideas for kids](https://diy.allwomenstalk.com/craft-ideas-for-kids/).

## 1. Ombre Tote Bag

![Ombre Tote Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f2/h3/y994w2v0_426x640.jpg)

Plain, canvas tote bags are a perfect way to show off your love for the ombre trend. It is a pretty simple ombre craft idea if you follow the tutorial over on [fortheloveof.net](https://goo.gl/Yc7DI4). Blogger Andrea used freezer paper to help stencil ombre hearts and lines onto her canvas tote bags. I cannot wait to try this out, now I just need a heart punch!

## 2. Ombre Vase

![Ombre Vase](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0b/jz/xet1xo3r_600x889.jpg)

There are some ombre looks out there that are pretty questionable, but when it is done correctly it can really make a difference to a plain and boring object. A clear glass vase or bottle is a great example. Painting the ombre effect on vases and bottles would be such a lovely idea for wedding centerpieces, especially for the spring and summer. This look can really make a difference, just check out the tutorial over on [cremedelacraft.com](http://www.cremedelacraft.com/2012/06/diy-ombre-vase-from-juice-jug.html), where blogger Natalie took an empty glass jug and gave it a beautiful new life with a little bit of paint and an ombre effect. You can also try a tutorial over on [modpodgerocksblog.com](https://modpodgerocksblog.com/how-to-decorate-a-vase-with-tissue-paper/) for a slightly different take on ombre vases.

## 3. Ombre Banister

![Ombre Banister](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a9/yw/kxr36t9j_333x500.jpg)

Some of these ideas are a bit more to take on than just a simple craft project and function more like a home improvement project. They are all amazing, though! Who would have thought an ombre staircase banister would be a trend? Not just the banister, but the stairs as well. I am in love with this look, and it is incredible how much difference a little bit of paint can make. From boring to chic and sophisticated, check out the tutorial over on [thisoldhouse.com](https://www.thisoldhouse.com/entryways-mudrooms) for the banister, and search for an ombre staircase on Pinterest for more amazing ideas. If you enjoy updating your space, you should also look at these fun [home decor projects](https://diy.allwomenstalk.com/home-decor-projects/).

## 4. Ombre Ballet Flats

![Ombre Ballet Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ig/nm/u9qpvqzt_570x855.jpg)

If you have a white or dull pink pair of ballet flats, or even a pair of white canvas shoes that need a little love, you are going to want to take some notes here. Seriously, I am loving each one of these ombre DIY ideas more than the one before it. This may be my favorite, so check out this ballet flat tutorial over on [swellmayde.com](https://goo.gl/WJ44CD). Simply adorable, right? Who wants to go shoe shopping for ballet flats with me?

## 5. Ombre Chalkboard

![Ombre Chalkboard](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iw/z8/0mfqss94_600x758.jpg)

Did you know that you could make your own chalkboard paint? Not only can you make your own chalkboard paint, but you can make it any color you want. This idea is relatively new to me, and even cooler than this idea is ombre chalkboard paint. Check out this gorgeous purple and pink ombre chalkboard that blogger Leslie put together at [freshfixdesign.com](https://goo.gl/On7EUj). It is simply stunning, and I am ready to get my craft on.

## 6. Ombre Lampshade

![Ombre Lampshade](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g4/la/jofazq26_600x1030.jpg)

This is not an ombre craft I would recommend doing with paint. You could, but it would be much easier to DIY with fabric. You could even use yarn to get your ombre effect like Mandi did over on [vintagerevivals.com](https://vintagerevivals.com/yarn-ombre-lampshade-tutorial). The one and only Martha Stewart also has an ombre lampshade tutorial on her official site using fabric shears. Both ideas work well, but if you really want to impress your guests, go with the yarn.

## 7. Ombre Candles

![Ombre Candles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jx/zu/vg1xa3j0_600x600.jpg)

Dress up those boring candles with some color and a cool ombre effect. This craft idea would make a great birthday gift for your mom, sister, or best friend. There are two ways you can make your own ombre candles. One tutorial I found over on [katescreativespace.com](http://www.katescreativespace.com/2013/06/25/neon-for-grown-ups/) just takes a pre-made candle stick and paints on a neon ombre look. However, there are many other guides out there that show you how to make soy wax ombre candles from scratch. Either way, they both make for pretty candles and great gift ideas.
