# 26 Fab Reasons to Hit up a Flea Market for Your Furniture ...

Do you love turning other people's trash into your own treasure? Me too! It's so awesome when you can buy something destined for the dump and give it a whole new life. It saves you money and gives you totally one-of-a-kind pieces for your home. If you're new to DIY projects of this kind, it's nice to have some inspiration to guide you. Check out these awesome items that were completely redone after being salvaged at the flea market. Garage sales are another cool place to find projects. Have fun!
