# DIY Home Remedies to Cure Dry Skin ...

While the cold season is perfect for coffee-and-cuddling moments, it's quite the hazard on the skin. For those suffering from dry skin, the following are great DIY remedies or you. Most of these dry skin beauty recipes call for ingredients that you can find in your own kitchen. Compared to store-bought dry skin remedies, these are healthier and more organic, too.

## 1. Yogurt Honey Face Mask

![Yogurt Honey Face Mask](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oh/vr/n19ga84x56d475e97d767036822809_400x600.jpg)

If the cold weather is being quite unfriendly to your face, this moisturizing mask will save you. The great thing about this DIY beauty recipe is that it's easy to make and uses ingredients that you already have at home. To make a moisturizing winter mask, you will need only Greek yogurt and raw honey.

Source: [eatthriveglow.com](http://www.eatthriveglow.com/yogurt-honey-face-mask/)

## 2. Almond Vanilla Body Oil

![Almond Vanilla Body Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yl/j8/iu6st1fk56d475ebc44c3071542913_500x750.jpg)

Here is a nice and organic alternative to your trusty lotion using only two ingredients --- almond oil and vanilla oil. You mix the two ingredients with the following ratio: 200ml of almond oil to every 5ml of vanilla oil. Shake well before applying to your skin.

Source: [coffeeandvanilla.com](https://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/almond-vanilla-body-oil/)

## 3. Oatmeal Honey Face Scrub

![Oatmeal Honey Face Scrub](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sb/vp/lyitiuf356d475ec33b09477140580_600x890.jpg)

This is a great dry skin beauty recipe that will also make your skin smell decadent. This scrub is perfect both for moisturizing and deep cleaning. Ingredients needed for this homemade scrub are finely ground oatmeal, honey, and sweet almond oil. Mix to a nice lather, apply and rub on face, and then rinse.

Source: [dabblesandbabbles.com](https://dabblesandbabbles.com/oatmeal-honey-face-scrub/)

## 4. Coconut Vanilla Body Scrub

![Coconut Vanilla Body Scrub](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ej/f0/cc2n0hje56d475ec6bb10534236975_531x800.jpg)

Here is a moisturizing DIY beauty concoction that will make you smell like your favorite tropical getaway. This body scrub requires only several ingredients. These are vanilla sugar, mineral sea salt, and extra virgin coconut oil. Vanilla beans are needed for making the vanilla sugar. However, vanilla essential oil can also be used for a much simpler recipe.

Source: [cookingalamel.com](http://cookingalamel.com/2013/03/moisturizing-coconut-vanilla-body-scrub.html)

## 5. Citrus Body Wash

![Citrus Body Wash](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c6/io/vjrjpl0l56d475ed6eb0f844957886_600x840.jpg)

Here is a wonderful homemade beauty product that you can use to fight dry skin in cold weather. I bet this will make your daily showers extra decadent. Combining castile soap with fractioned coconut oil and other essential oils, this body wash is the moisturizing homemade product that you've been looking for all winter.

Source: [helloglow.co](https://helloglow.co/moisturizing-citrus-homemade-body-wash/)

## 6. Poppy Seed and Goat's Milk Soap

![Poppy Seed and Goat's Milk Soap](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r6/k9/jt5dhr0d56d475ee49c80508634341_600x900.jpg)

This DIY soap recipe has ingredients that is not only fantastic for treating dry skin, they're are also great for exfoliation and making your skin glow. The moisturizing parts will be handled by the goat's milk soap base. Other ingredients needed are grapefruit, poppy seeds, and essential oils.

Source: [abeautifulmess.com](https://abeautifulmess.com/make-your-own-soap-grapefruit-mint-poppyseed-bars/)

## 7. Peppermint Sugar Scrub

![Peppermint Sugar Scrub](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/te/kg/l1evebwb56d475ed68f36173404836_600x836.jpg)

Want a winter dry skin remedy that's also good for relieving muscle pain? Then this peppermint sugar scrub is for you. This is an easy beauty recipe with ingredients that you can find in your own kitchen. To make your own winter-perfect scrub, you will need only sugar, coconut oil, and peppermint essential oil. Mix and apply to your dry and achy body.

Source: [sugarandsoul.co](https://www.sugarandsoul.co/peppermint-sugar-scrub-recipe/)

## 8. Lemon Lip Scrub

![Lemon Lip Scrub](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fp/vf/aggkc0o956d475ed3476e765896732_599x898.jpg)

Dry lips is never pretty. One way to combat the situation is to exfoliate religiously. And the only way to do it properly is to make your very own moisturizing lip scrub. We love this recipe with a nice lemon-y essence. Other ingredients needed are jojoba oil, raw honey, and sugar.

Source: [apumpkinandaprincess.com](https://apumpkinandaprincess.com/diy-exfoliating-lemon-lip-scrub)

Go ahead and pamper yourself with these DIY dry skin beauty products. Whip up a batch for you and give some away to your friends.
