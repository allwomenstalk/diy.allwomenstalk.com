Frequently Asked Questions

Is it okay to make something for my crush for Valentine's Day?
Totally! It's super sweet to put in the effort to make something by hand. It shows you care and adds a personal touch that just can't be bought. Go for it!
I'm not super crafty; can I still make a Valentine's gift for my crush?
Of course! It's the thought and the effort that counts. There are loads of simple and easy things you can make that don't require you to be the next Martha Stewart. Just choose something from the heart, and your crush will love it.
What's a good surprise for my crush on Valentine's Day?
Ooh, surprises are the best! Think about a small gift paired with a cute note, a jar filled with reasons why you like them, or even a playlist of songs that remind you of them. Whatever you choose, it's the surprise factor that'll give them butterflies!
Should I go big with my Valentine's Day gift for my crush?
Hold up! Remember, bigger isn't always better. It's about the gesture, so keep it cute and meaningful. A gigantic teddy bear might be a bit much. Think more along the lines of a handmade card, a batch of cookies, or a little book of memories you’ve shared.
What if I'm worried my crush won't like my homemade gift?
Hey, don't stress! If you put your heart into making something special, that's what really matters. It's genuine and from you. If your crush is the right kind of person, they'll appreciate the gift no matter what it is because it came from you.
