# 23 Lovely DIY Valentines Day Wreaths ...

We have another post filled to the brim with V-Day crafts; this time, we tackle DIY Valentine's Day Wreaths. If the banners are helpful for making your mantel (or any wall in your home, for that matter) more season-appropriate, these DIY Valentine wreaths will take care of your door. Let's check out these fun DIYs, shall we?

## 1. Mini Heart Banner and Yarn

![Mini Heart Banner and Yarn](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2013/01/110-560x465_560x465.jpg)

This is an easy-to-make DIY Valentine's Day wreath that will require a wreath form, yarn, scissors, felt, hot glue, buttons, ribbon, and scrapbook paper. For the yarn, you will need two colors for a wonderful stripe-inspired look.

[Tutorial](http://www.measureonce-cuttwice.com/2012/01/diy-valentines-day-wreath.html)

## 2. Burlap and Felt Roses

![Burlap and Felt Roses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2013/01/210_480x374.jpg)

This is a wreath idea that you have probably seen in other variations. Basically, to make this, you will wrap a wreath form with burlap and embellish with roses made of felt. Easy but pretty!

[Tutorial](http://olearyva.blogspot.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. Argyle

![Argyle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2013/01/39_500x452.jpg)

If preppy is more your style, then you will absolutely love this argyle motif V-Day wreath. The materials that you will need to make one for your door are a wreath form, gray yarn, felt (in red and white), hot glue, and scissors. Check out the link below for the assembly steps.

[Tutorial](http://memoirs0famama.blogspot.com/2012/01/diy-valentines-day-argyle-wreath.html)

## 4. Pom Poms and Yarn Balls

![Pom Poms and Yarn Balls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2013/01/410_400x267.jpg)

How cute! I love this because it's incredibly colorful and also because it looks like the perfect wreath for a craft room door.

[Tutorial](http://buggieandjellybean.blogspot.com/2011/01/valentines-wreath-diy.html)

## 5. Stenciled Wooden Hearts

Tutorial_

What's a Valentine's Day decor without alluding to hugs and kisses, right? So here we are with a lovely wreath that says XOXO. Hopefully, everyone who sees this ends up giving you a hug and a kiss.

## 12. Curly Grosgrain

## 13. Sweet Valentine's Day Wreath

## 14. Ruffled Heart

![Ruffled Heart](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eb/vr/c6xsamfu54cbb1ffb62b4657823355_492x500.jpg)

[Tutorial](http://partywishesscv.blogspot.com/2011/01/ruffled-heart.html)

## 15. Paper Heart Wreath

![Paper Heart Wreath](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xq/q4/up18wnvt54cbb20008d9e521116130_600x600.jpg)
