# 7 DIY Videos about Home Dcor on a Budget ...

I love videos about home décor a lot. By a lot, I mean that I could spend hours watching them if I had the time. Because I do not, and I assume that you do not as well, I’ve compiled the best DIY videos about home décor to help you decorate your place, whether you’re on a budget or simply looking for some new DIY projects! No matter what your goal is, you’ll undoubtedly find some of your new favorite DIY projects!

## 1. Upcycle Your Used Candle Jars with Ingrid

There are so many tips and tricks about upcycling used candle jars when it comes to videos about home décor, but this is one of my favorites that I’ve come across so far. I love the idea of using it as a nightlight, especially if you’re looking for something for your little one’s bedroom. I also think they’d make the perfect outdoor décor for a summer night with friends!

## 2. Easy DIY Jewelry/Accessory Organizers with LaurDIY

I’ve spent the past few months obsessed with finding new and cute ways to display and store my jewelry, and this video is one of the best that I’ve found. I love the quirky knobs she uses in the second tutorial. I often see organizers like that on Pinterest, but I never thought to make it myself and save myself from the big price tag!

## 3. DIY Chic Wall Art and Polaroid Display with AnneOrShine

A few weeks ago I spent almost an entire Saturday watching all of Ann’s videos, and it has been downhill ever since. This DIY is one of my favorites, and one of the easiest, in my opinion. I constantly find myself with Polaroids that I want to display, but I’m not quite sure how to do so. I will definitely be employing this DIY when I move into my new place!

## 4. DIY Creative Colorful Wall Art with AnneOrShine

This video from Ann is one of my favorite DIY videos on YouTube, and I’ve watched a lot of DIY videos on YouTube. If you’re looking to create a gallery wall in your house but don’t want to get stuck with a ridiculous price tag, look no further than this video. Ann will show you how to create gorgeous, colorful wall art for a fraction of the price!

## 5. How to Make Faux Fur Stools with AFabLife

Although this DIY requires you to be a little bit of a scavenger, the end result will be worth it. This is the perfect DIY if you’re looking to add some polish to your home without breaking the bank. This piece looks chic and refined, even if you made it yourself with thrift store stools!

## 6. How to Create an Easy Gallery Wall with AFabLife

This video is perfect for the moment after you make all of your wall art with Ann’s help. It can be daunting trying to figure out how to position everything on your gallery wall, but the girls from AFabLife will help you in almost no time!

## 7. Stenciled Accent Pillows with Lauren Conrad

I’m a sucker for stenciled accent pillows, I’ll admit. I think that they’re such an easy way to jazz up boring pillows! Let Lauren Conrad teach you how the next time you’re looking to turn your pillows into stenciled masterpieces!

What’s your favorite DIY home décor video? Let me know in the comments, I’m always looking for new DIY ideas!
