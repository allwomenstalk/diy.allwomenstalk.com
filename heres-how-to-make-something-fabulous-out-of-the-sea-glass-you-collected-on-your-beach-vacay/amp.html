Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is sea glass?
Sea glass is basically broken glass that's been tumbled and smoothed by the ocean over many years. It turns into these lovely, frosted, colorful little gems. Kind of like nature’s way of recycling, right?
How do I clean my sea glass before using it?
Oh, it's super easy! Just soak the sea glass in soapy water for a bit, then use an old toothbrush to scrub off any dirt or residue. Rinse it with fresh water, and you're good to go!
What kind of crafts can I make with sea glass?
Honestly, the sky's the limit! You can make stunning jewelry, decorate picture frames, create unique wall art, or even make pretty mosaic pieces. It’s such a versatile material—just let your imagination run wild!
Do I need special tools for sea glass crafts?
Not really! A few basics like glue, jewelry wire, and maybe a pair of pliers will get you started. If you're getting into more complex projects like drilling holes in sea glass, you might need a small rotary tool. But start small and keep it fun!
Can I mix sea glass with other materials in my crafts?
Absolutely! Sea glass looks fantastic when paired with other natural materials like driftwood, shells, or even regular stones. Mixing different textures and colors can make your projects really stand out.
