# 38 Creative Nautical Decor Ideas & DIY Coastal Decorations for Any Home

The nautical beach trend brings breezy coastal energy and relaxed maritime charm into any living space. Even if you do not live in a beach cottage, you can easily incorporate beach rope decor, sea glass accents, and outdoor coastal decor ideas into your house regardless of your location. Whether you want to refresh a master suite, style a backyard patio, or discover creative boat decor ideas, here is all the inspiration you need for your next DIY home refresh.

## 1. Fill Candle Holders with Sand and Shells

![Fill Candle Holders with Sand and Shells](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ei/ax/i931dilz5547974ab4246455872455_600x798.jpg)

Bring the seaside indoors by filling simple glass hurricane candle holders with fine beach sand and collected seashells. Nestling a pillar candle inside creates a warm, coastal centerpiece for your dining table or patio table.

## 2. Make a Wreath with Yarn and Twine

![Make a Wreath with Yarn and Twine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cf/dm/hh5guw115547974be7cbc172794496_500x750.jpg)

A handmade wreath wrapped in ocean-blue yarn and natural twine makes a charming front door accent. For a full tutorial, check out this creative [DIY nautical wreath design](https://makingtheworldcuter.com/nautical-decor-diy-wreath/) to greet your guests with coastal flair.

## 3. Use Ship Cleats for Window Treatment Hooks

![Use Ship Cleats for Window Treatment Hooks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ti/q6/nl76lj2q5547974c07736868583145_298x500.jpg)

Swap standard curtain hardware for authentic metal or wooden boat cleats to hold back drapes. This practical idea works wonderfully for boat decor ideas as well as coastal living rooms.

## 4. Build a Custom Shelving Unit with Rope and Wood

![Build a Custom Shelving Unit with Rope and Wood](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mk/ym/icauehbg5547974c5a89f959704197_475x749.jpg)

Thread thick natural manila rope through pre-drilled wooden planks to create floating open shelves. It adds storage while embodying rustic nautical decorating.

## 5. Cover a Coastal Candle Holder in Twine and Glitter

![Cover a Coastal Candle Holder in Twine and Glitter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ed/w4/q6ju1e1k5547974ca9f0c589598736_600x774.jpg)

Wrap jute twine around plain mason jars and accent them with subtle metallic glitter or tiny starfish. Explore more [creative nautical home decorating ideas](https://hative.com/creative-nautical-home-decorating-ideas/) to craft glowing ambiance.

- **Natural Elements:** Incorporate sand, sea glass, and shells for organic coastal textures.

- **Tactile Fabrics & Ropes:** Use heavy cotton, canvas, and Manila rope to build maritime warmth.

- **Harmonious Color Palette:** Pair navy blue and crisp white with soft sandy neutrals and seafoam accents.

## 6. Put Natural Rope in a Vase or Jar

![Put Natural Rope in a Vase or Jar](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rn/zw/mrrn92q45547974ce350d969435653_450x328.jpg)

Coiling thick beach rope decor inside clear glass cylinders is one of the easiest diy nautical decorations. For inspiration on craft projects, browse [decorating nautical theme guides](https://decor4all.com/enhancing-nautical-decor-theme-sea-shell-crafts-images/24925/).

## 7. Paint a Bookcase with Coastal Colors and Add Nautical Accessories

![Paint a Bookcase with Coastal Colors and Add Nautical Accessories](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ui/s6/mytiu4jx5547974d66919048973206_600x902.jpg)

Give an old bookshelf a fresh coat of chalk paint in navy blue or soft aqua, then style the shelves with coral branches and vintage brass compasses. Learn step-by-step techniques in this [bookcase makeover guide](https://www.craftsbycourtney.com/how-to-crafts/annie-sloan-bookcase-makeover/).

## 8. Go Easy with Sand in Jars

![Go Easy with Sand in Jars](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qg/bv/dbjdukhm5547974d4ba11116663509_320x481.jpg)

For a minimalist look, layer different shades of sand inside glass jars or votives. You can cross-reference this with our [coastal candle holder project](#5) or pair it with [natural rope in a jar](#6) for a simple shelf display.

## 9. Hang Frames from String

![Hang Frames from String](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ys/rh/rxqjrhol5547974d70d1a065012812_400x484.jpg)

Suspend picture frames using sturdy jute twine attached to wooden wall pegs. Discover how to display seaside memories with these [summer seashell decor ideas](https://www.simplydesigning.net/10-summer-seashell-decor-ideas) and complement them with [diy coastal wall art ideas](#30).

## 10. Build Wood Marine Pilings for Your Outdoor Space

![Build Wood Marine Pilings for Your Outdoor Space](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xk/cg/j4fxnn9s5547974d9db20420106314_332x400.jpg)

Group wooden posts of varying heights and wrap them in thick rope to craft decorative nautical pilings with rope. This is one of the most striking outdoor nautical decor ideas for gardens, porches, or patio steps, adding genuine harbor charm to your nautical landscaping ideas.

## 11. Tie Back Your Curtains with Rope

![Tie Back Your Curtains with Rope](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qf/il/b7eeflid5547974de1e41722582188_570x859.jpg)

Heavy knotted cotton rope tiebacks lend an effortless maritime feel to linen drapes. You can find pre-knotted options on [Etsy](https://www.etsy.com) or tie your own monkey-fist knots. This pairs perfectly with [ship cleat curtain hooks](#3).

## 12. Use a Wooden Pallet as a Backdrop for Your Sea Accessories

![Use a Wooden Pallet as a Backdrop for Your Sea Accessories](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cv/gv/hbw6x6yo5547974e22008487860606_480x370.jpg)

Mount a weathered wooden pallet on your wall to display wooden oars, starfish, and metal anchors. Explore clever upcycling tips in this collection of [wood pallet decor ideas](https://www.completely-coastal.com/2012/07/diy-wood-pallet-decor-ideas.html).

## 13. Add a Lovely Driftwood Wheel

![Add a Lovely Driftwood Wheel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ls/vl/vnewbgcr5547974e4df13477050641_345x512.jpg)
