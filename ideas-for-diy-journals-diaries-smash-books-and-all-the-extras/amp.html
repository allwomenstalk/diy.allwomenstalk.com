Frequently Asked Questions

What materials do I need to start a DIY journal?
Basic supplies like blank notebooks, decorative paper, glue, scissors, and pens are great to begin with.
How can I personalize my diary to make it unique?
Add stickers, photos, washi tape, and your own drawings or lettering to reflect your style.
What’s a smash book and how is it different from a journal?
A smash book is a creative collection of mementos and notes, less structured than a journal, focusing on memories and scrapbooking.
Can I use recycled materials for my DIY journals?
Yes! Using recycled paper, old magazines, and scraps can add a unique touch and is eco-friendly.
How often should I update my DIY journal or diary?
There’s no rule—write or craft whenever you feel inspired, whether daily, weekly, or just occasionally.
What are some fun themes for DIY journals?
Themes like travel, gratitude, dreams, or wellness can make journaling more focused and enjoyable.
How can I make my journal pages more durable?
Use thicker paper, laminate covers, or add protective sprays to keep your pages looking fresh.
Are there apps to help me design journal layouts before making a DIY journal?
Yes, apps like Canva or GoodNotes help plan layouts, which you can then replicate by hand.
How do smash books help with creativity?
They encourage playful collecting, combining art, writing, and keepsakes, sparking new creative ideas.
What’s a quick DIY idea to add to my journal?
Try a pocket page to store tickets, notes, or small keepsakes for a personal touch.
