2 Personalized Mug

Tutorial

This is one of the cheapest DIY Valentine’s Day gift you can make, but it may also be the sweetest. You can buy plain white mugs from most dollar stores and personalize them with any phrase, lyrics or quotes that symbolize your love for your sweetie. Write anything you would like on your mug. My husband and I got married last February and our first dance song was "Lucky" by Jason Mraz and Colbie Caillat, so I wrote a lyric to the song on a mug for him. He absolutely loved it! It makes him think about our wedding every time he uses the mug.

***

This DIY Valentine's Day gift is perfect for any man in your life. Not only is it a great way to show your love, but it's also a cheap and easy way to make a unique gift. All you need is a plain white mug from the dollar store and a permanent marker. Write a special message, quote, or lyric to symbolize your love for your sweetheart.
