# Party Hats That Are Cute and Creative ...

Whether it's New Years Eve or a birthday, it's not a party without party hats; but they're childish right? Not anymore! Head on out to the nearest craft store and gather the supplies you need. Today we will check out festive party hats you can make before that huge shindig. Let's get crafting!

## 1. Two-toned

_ I love these two-toned** party hats**. This is a clever tutorial brought to us by Martha Stewart. To make these, you will need decorative paper, vellum, double-sided tape, pencil, round plates as template, white glue, and ribbon. You can skip the vellum if you use pretty card stock.

Tutorial_

## 2. Ruffled

![Ruffled](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/12/211-560x329_560x329.jpg)

These are incredibly pretty, too. And oh-so-easy to make! If you have lots of fabric scraps, you can use them for making the ruffled hems of these** party hats**. Instead of using patterned card stock, you can also use glittery paper.

[Tutorial](https://asubtlerevelry.com/handcrafted-party-ruffled-style/)

## 3. Peter Pan

![Peter Pan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/12/311_550x437.jpg)

This is a cute idea, especially if the party that you're planning is Rated PG. Check out the tutorial on how to make the hat and the feather embellishment. The latter is adorable and I think I will copy it to make other fun craft projects.

[Tutorial](http://swoonstudio.blogspot.com/2012/03/peter-pan-party-hats.html)

## 4. Mini Hats

![Mini Hats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/12/4a-560x423_560x423.jpg)

These are tiny **party hats** that you can make for your upcoming soiree. To make these, you simply cut a store-bought party hat into a smaller accessory, attach it to a headband, and embellish as you like. The shinier, the better.

[Tutorial](http://www.hernewleaf.com/2012/01/27/diy-mini-birthday-party-hat-fascinator/)

## 5. Fancy Top Hats

![Fancy Top Hats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/12/510_500x333.jpg)

How awesome are these? These look very fancy but they are actually very easy to make. The glittery hat is made of glitter foam but you can also use any shiny paper or card stock you can find. For embellishment, you have plenty of options: ribbons, feathers, buttons, beads, felt, tiny toys, and many more.

[Tutorial](https://web.archive.org/web/https://thealisonshow.com/2011/09/fancy-party-hat-tutorial.html)

## 6. Floral

![Floral](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/12/611_423x308.jpg)

These are absolutely darling. These pretty flowers are sold at craft stores. For the hat base, you can make your own using card stock or paper.

[Tutorial](https://web.archive.org/web/https://mayarts.com/tutorial-lil-party-hat/)

## 7. Glitter

![Glitter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/12/710_450x320.jpg)

Here we have another party hat idea perfect for a party. You will be covering the cone with glitter yourself in making these hats. However, if you can find card stock that is already shiny and pretty, you can skip a few steps.

[Tutorial](https://studiodiy.com/diy-glitter-holiday-party-hats/)

## 8. Crepe Flowers

![Crepe Flowers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/12/89-560x375_560x375.jpg)

This is another flora idea but equally as cute. This is actually more like a fascinator but you can easily ask your party guests to don these.

[Tutorial](https://ohhappyday.com/2011/11/diy-floral-party-hats/)

## 9. Accordion

![Accordion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/diy/2012/12/910-560x408_560x408.jpg)

These look like something a member of the British Royal family will wear to a formal daytime event. They're cute and cheap party hats to make and will look fab in pics.

[Tutorial](https://asubtlerevelry.com/accordion-party-hats/)

## 10. Chalkboard

![Party hat, Witch hat, Party supply,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ro/a2/rzd0tjf85d80f94a3450e800150874_560x362.jpg)

This is a fantastic way to make personalized **party hats**. To make these, you will paint cardboard party hats with chalkboard paint (2 or 3 coats will do) and then decorate with tinsel and other embellishments. Easy and fun!

[Tutorial](http://prettysouthern.com/2011/08/01/d-i-y-chalkboard-party-hat/)
